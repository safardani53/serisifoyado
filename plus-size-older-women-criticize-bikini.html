A PLUS-SIZE woman has shared her unbothered response to people who criticize her appearance.

The influencer showed off her curves in her favorite bikini despite being told to cover up.

TikTok user Payton (@paytonfore_) explained that so-called Karens often comment on her body.

"When the 60-year-old women in my comments tell me ‘plus-size girls shouldn’t wear a two-piece swimsuit’ but you do anyway," Payton wrote in her clip.

The curvy creator posed in a $22 underwire purple bikini top and the matching $15 bikini bottoms from Target.

In a previous video, the TikToker referred to the bikini as her favorite look from her extensive swimwear collection.

Payton politely responded to the online trolls in the caption of her video.

"Thanks for your opinion, Carol. I’m gonna wear it anyway," she said.

"Grannies are unhinged these days," the content creator joked in the comment section.

The fashion fan regularly posts body-positive content as well as sharing her various shopping hauls.
