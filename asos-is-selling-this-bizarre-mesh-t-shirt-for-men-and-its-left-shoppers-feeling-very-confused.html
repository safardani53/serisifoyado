ASOS is selling this bizarre mesh 'T-shirt' for men... and it's left shoppers feeling VERY confused

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, July 6, 2024

2017 is the year of bold fashion - and the bonkers trends don't look like they're going anywhere any time soon.

ASOS has upped their quirky clothing game by releasing a VERY bizarre T-shirt.

The statement clothing item is described on the retailer's website as an Extreme Longline T-Shirt In Loose Mesh.

The £22 bold garment clearly won't be to everyone's taste.

Not only is it double the length of a normal T-shirt, it is also fashioned from revealing mesh material.

Even though the shin-length top covers most of the body, there's no chance that it'll keep you warm this winter.

This may be because the design has plenty of holes in and leaves nothing to the imagination.

The model on the site thankfully has some denim shorts beneath the tee to protect his modesty.

Unsurprisingly, social media users have been left feeling baffled by the unusual fashion item.

One Scottish shopper even took to Twitter to ask: "Wtf ASOS".

It’s not the first time that the label has confused customers with their style choices.

This super cropped jumper has been the talk of the internet this week.

Earlier this month, they released this bizarre Nineties-style denim skirt AND jeans combination.

This unusual colour block top also sparked discussion.

And this bag modelled on the poo emoji didn’t go down very well.
