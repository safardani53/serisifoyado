Mauricio Umansky Cheating On Kyle Richards? Is RHOBH Marriage In Trouble? Hollywood Li

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, June 16, 2024

Mauricio Umansky Cheating On Kyle Richards?

“There are tons of stories circulating about his extra-marital activities – it is common knowledge in their social circles. The only person who doesn’t know is Kyle, she is so proud of her sexy husband, but if she ever found out about his flings, it would destroy her,” sources told Star.

Tess Broussard of Comedy Central’s Kroll Show told Star about an encounter she had a few years ago with Mauricio. They were at the Beverly Wilshire Hotel with her friend, Cristina Schultz. “Mauricio just beelined for us,” the source added. After a flirty conversation, he allegedly said, “I’m married, but I stray once in a while.”

However, HollywoodLife.com has learned exclusively that, “Mauricio is a very good looking guy and people assume his warm nature and good looks to be an invite to something more but he is not cheating on Kyle, the claims are laughable.”

Our source adds, “He is not going to ruin his marriage or his brand with cheating, he’s in love with Kyle and he’s not stupid.”

HollywoodLife.com has reached out to Kyle & Mauricio’s reps and we are waiting for comment, but they did tell Star that Mauricio is “networking and being friendly and pleasant, and this is construed as flirting. He is a good looking guy.”

HollywoodLifers, did you ever believe that Mauricio was cheating on Kyle? Sound off below!
