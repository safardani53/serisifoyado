Varshini Sounderajan, also known as Shamili Sounderajan, is a Telugu cinema actress and television presenter from India. She is well known for her roles as Sathya in the Telugu romance film “Nannu Dochukunduvate” (2018) and Jagadamba in the web series “Pelli Gola” (2017), both of which she co-starred with Abijeet Duddala.

Career

  • Varshini Sounderajan began her career as a model before appearing in the Telugu film “Shambo Shiva Shambo” as a cameo (2010).

  • Following that, she appeared in other Telugu films, including Lovers (2014), Kaai Raja Kaai (2015), Best Actors (2015), Sri Rama Raksha (2016), and Nannu Dochukunduvate (2018).
  • In 2017, she was named the Hyderabad Times’ Most Desirable Woman on Television.
  • She also appeared in the online series “Pelli Gola” as the main Jagadamba opposite Abijeet Duddala. She later featured in Pelli Gola seasons 2 and 3.
