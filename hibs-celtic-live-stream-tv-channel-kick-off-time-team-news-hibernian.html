Hibs vs Celtic LIVE stream, TV channel, team news and kick-off time for Scottish Premiership

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, January 1, 0001

CELTIC make the trip to Hibernian as they look to tighten their grip on the Scottish Premiership title.

With Rangers slipping up last time out, Brendan Rodgers' men are looking to build on their emphatic 5-1 victory over Kilmarnock.

Is Hibernian vs Celtic on TV?

THIS Scottish Premiership clash is all set for Sunday, December 16.

Kick-off is at 12.30pm.

When the sides met earlier this season, Celtic ran out 4-2 winners after an entertaining clash at Parkhead.

Which TV channel can I watch it on?

YOU can catch this game live on Sky Sports Football.

Coverage begins at 12pm, whilst you can stream without a Sky subscription by purchasing a NOW TV pass from £7.99.

And as ever, SunSport will be running a LIVE BLOG to keep you up to date with all the action as it happens.
