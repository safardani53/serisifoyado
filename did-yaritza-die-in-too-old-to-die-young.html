Did Yaritza die in Too Old to Die Young?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, May 29, 2024

THE hit show Too Old to Die Young wowed viewers when it was released in 2019.

But the season finale has left many confused as questions remain unanswered - one being if main character Yaritza died.

Did Yaritza die in Too Old to Die Young?

The short answer to this question is no - Yaritza did not die in Too Old to Die Young.

Yaritza won over viewers for her role as the "High Priestess of Death", a woman who's on a warpath against the cartels in the show.

Yaritza was on a mission to kill as many cartel captors as she could while freeing their sex slaves.

How did Too Old to Die Young end?

The first season of Too Old to Die Young ended at the site of another massacre orchestrated by Yaritza.

After killing all the men in the establishment, she leaves, hinting that she'll continue her mission to free sex slaves and slaughter their captors.

But her whereabouts remain unknown, leaving room for a second season.

Fans went wild with conspiracy theories after the series ended.

One posted: "Things are coming to a head. There's no way anyone makes it out alive, even Yaritza - the deadliest of them all!"

Another praised the character: "This is exactly what prestige TV is meant to be and never is. Yaritza is the best original character of the 21st century so far!"

One fan simply observed: "I think it's a pity that there is no second season. I would have been interested in how Yaritza proceeded. There are still a few unanswered questions for me."

Who played Yaritza and what else has Cristina Rodlo been in?

Yaritza was played by Mexican actress Cristina Rodlo, from Torreon.

She's been in many television shows and movies, most notably being Too Old to Die Young.

She also appeared in The Condemned, El Vato, 68 Whiskey, Miss Bala, Bunker and No One Gets Out Alive.

The 32-year old has been acting since she was young, first beginning her career at the age of 18 in Verano 79.

Who is in the cast of Too Old to Die Young?

The hit television show on Amazon Prime had a star studded cast - ranging from Miles Teller to Jena Malone.

Babs Olusanmokun played Damian, along with Nell Tiger Free portraying Janey Carter.

John Hawkes played Viggo Larsen, and Augusto Aguilera portrayed Jesus Rojas.
