Halle Berry gets in the festive spirit as she wows in a bright red dress

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, May 8, 2024

IT’S beginning to look a hot like Christmas as Halle Berry wows fans in this bright red dress.

The actress headed a list of famous names as she attended the fourth annual Celebration of Black Cinema & Television in Los Angeles.

Halle, 55, received the Career Achievement Award and used her speech to praise her fella, singer Van Hunt, 51.

She said: “I finally found love this year.

“I found the love of my life and I know it’s true because when you fail so many times you know what wrong looks like.

“My partner is here tonight, Van, and you have completed me.

"You lifted me higher and made me more of myself.”

Halle chatted to Demi Singleton, 14, and Saniyya Sidney, 15, who played the Williams ­tennis sisters in the movie King Richard, based on the girls’ dad and starring Will Smith.
