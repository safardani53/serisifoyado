I thought losing 70kg would make me happy, but Im left with loose skin on my tummy that flaps aro

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 2, 2024

A WOMAN has confessed she’s been left with saggy skin and breasts following a dramatic weight loss. 

Rhiannon Reid often uses her social media to give insight into her life, including her three-year journey to losing 70kg. 

In one video, she spoke openly about the struggles she’s faced following her journey - especially with having excess skin.

“Me sad with saggy boobs and skin,” she wrote while lip syncing a voice about cows.

“Realising that when you lose 70kg that’s just what happens”. 

In the caption, she added: “So disheartening some days, but then I see how far I have come,” followed by a shrugging emoji.

People were quick to comment as one person asked: “sorry if this is touchy but how long did it take you? what weight did you start at? you look amazing btw”.

TikTok user @_sandra15__ replied: “No not at all! I weighted 150 kilos and it took 3 years, thank you beautiful,” followed by a red heart emoji. 

Another said: “I hear you baby,” to which she replied: “How annoying is it,” followed by a crying with laughter emoji. 

A third compared her to Drew Barrymore, while a fourth asked her how she dealt with the loose skin.

Replying to this comment in a section video, she shared: “Still have my skin sadly, I do want surgery but its expensive asf,” with a crying emoji 

People were left inspired by how open she is about her weight loss.

One person said: “You are so beautiful, and you give me strength to love myself as I am and also to take care of me, as you do for yourself, thank you,” with a black heart emoji.

A second posted: “Thank you for showing this. I lost big amount of weight and felt so alone with my skin. Seeing people like me, makes it easier to accept”.

While a third added: “you have done amazing such determination, i love it! x”.
