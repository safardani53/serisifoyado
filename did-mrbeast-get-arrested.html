Did MrBeast get arrested? | The US Sun

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, August 18, 2024

BELOVED YouTube creator MrBeast worried fans in May 2023 after pictures of him getting arrested went viral.

Here's what we know about the pictures and his arrest.

Did MrBeast get arrested?

In May 2023, Jimmy Donaldson, aka MrBeast, was pulled over in North Carolina while driving a Tesla.

At the time, cops told him that the traffic stop was for his tinted windows, however, after running his divers license, an arrest warrant popped up over "communicating threats," according to the Daily Mail.

Cops then proceeded to handcuff and transport him to a holding cell- where he was left without food or water.

At first, the news of MrBeast's arrest worried fans, but it was later revealed that the stunt was a prank pulled off by fellow YouTuber Airrack.

Airrack was reportedly getting back at MrBeast for a prank he had pulled a year earlier.

A video of Airrack's prank was posted on Youtube and received over 4.6million views in one day.

The prank was so good, that even MrBeast himself was worried.

"I was like, this might be a prank. I was like, this is a lot of resources for a prank. When I got in there I was like, Okay wait. Maybe this isn't a prank," MrBeast said.

What have fans said about the video?

After Airrack posted the video online, several fans took to the comments section to voice their opinions.

"You know it's legit when Jimmy actually swears in a video," one user commented.

"This was awesome," another added.

"This isn’t just one of Airracks best videos…. It’s one of the best on all of YouTube. Can’t wait to see what Jimmy does back and the series of you prancing other YouTubers. This needs to be monthly thing at least," a third said.

While most viewers found the video funny, others were upset about the use of police resources, however, DailyMail notes that the officers in the clip were off-duty and Airrack paid the Pitt County Sheriff's Office for "contract work."

Who is Airrack?

Eric Decker is a YouTuber who goes by the name of Airrack online.

He was born on January 12, 1997, and is arguably best known for his  “couch series” collaboration with Logan Paul.

"I’m just a kid with a dream," his YouTube bio states.

Airrack joined the video-sharing platform in January 2015 and has since received over 1.9billion views and over 13.2million subscribers.

His content consists of prank videos as well as attempts at breaking world records.

In January 2023, Airrack received the Guinness World Record for the largest pizza after making one that measured in at 13,990 square feet.
