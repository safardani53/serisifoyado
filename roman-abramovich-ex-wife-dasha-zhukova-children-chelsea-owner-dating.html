Who is Roman Abramovich's ex-wife Dasha, how many children do they have, and who is the Chelsea owne

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, March 27, 2024

ROMAN ABRAMOVICH has had a busy, but frustrating year so far - as he tries to sell Chelsea amid visa issues.

But now his problems have gone up a level, as the New York Post revealed his ex-wife was found at the New York mansion of his business partner Oleg Deripaska, after it was seized by the FBI.

Who is Roman Abramovich's ex-wife?

Dasha Zhukova was born June 8, 1971 in Moscow, Russia.

The 51-year-old Chelsea owner married the daughter of Russian oligarch Alexander Zhukov in 2008.

But the pair announced they were separating in August last year.

The 37-year-old has many interests, being a businesswoman, art collector and magazine editor.

With the help of her former husband, Zhukova set up the Garage Museum of Contemporary Art in Moscow.

How many children do they have?

Abramovich has a son, Aaron Alexander, and a daughter, Leah Lou, with Zhukova with both born in the United States where she also holds citizenship.

The Chelsea owner also has five children from his relationship with former wife Irina Vyacheslavovna Malandina - Ilya, Arina, Sofia, Arkadiy and Anna.

Who is Abramovich currently dating?

Abramovich has been divorced three times, ending a three-year marriage with Olga Yurevna Lysova, prior to his relationships with Malandina and Zhukova.

But accordign to the New York Post, the Chelsea owner is now dating Polina Deripaska, the estranged wife of his business partner Oleg.

Meanwhile, his ex-wife Zhukova has been seeing Greek shipping heir Stavros Niarchos.
