Gal Gadots Best Red Carpet Fashion, Dress Moments

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, June 14, 2024

Credit: Shutterstock

See Gal Gadot's Wondrous Red Carpet Style Evolution

Gal Gadot has superhero-worthy style! From her kick-butt role in Fast and Furious to her career-defining turn as Wonder Woman, we are in awe of the brunette beauty’s grace, strength and talent both on and off screen.

And with a string of blockbuster hits under her belt, the former Miss Israel has wowed Us with her sophisticated-yet-sexy red carpet beauty and fashion sense.

Whether she is dripping in diamonds at the Oscars or rocking a HBIC-worthy black tux at a movie premiere, the star always seems to be having fun with her fashion choices.

She is often shimmering on the red carpet in bright, colorful designs adorned with sequins and beads. Need we remind you of the sparkling scarlet Givenchy gown she wore to the L.A. premiere of Wonder Woman in May 2017?

And her bewitching beauty looks (think: flawless skin, bold lips, sculptural hairstyles) only add to the glam.

[ami-related id="2713" url="https://www.usmagazine.com/stylish/pictures/best-oscar-dresses-of-all-time-2012222/" title="See the Best Oscar Dresses of Years Past" target="_blank" thumb="false" imgsrc="https://www.usmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2020/02/Oscars-2020-Arrivals-Janelle-Monae-Slide.jpg?quality=86&strip=all" imgid="1719838" format="gallery" flag="" channel="33"]

The Revlon ambassador, who regularly works with makeup artist Sabrina Bedrani, tends to keep her complexion glowing with a subtly sun-kissed contour. It serves as the perfect dewy base to experiment with fun pops of color on her eyes, lips (a cherry pout has become somewhat of a signature for her) and cheeks.

On the hair front, she has rocked everything from sleek strands to intricate updos. But no matter the style, mane man Renato Campora keeps her raven-colored strands shining bright like a diamond. Seriously, have you seen those lustrous locks?

[ami-related id="1360560" url="https://www.usmagazine.com/stylish/pictures/julia-roberts-best-red-carpet-fashion-dresses-suits/" title="See Julia Roberts' Best Red Carpet Fashion Moments" target="_blank" thumb="false" imgsrc="https://www.usmagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2018/10/julia-roberts3.jpg?quality=86&strip=all" imgid="1359567" format="gallery" flag="" channel="33"]

Over the years, Gadot has had fun with her looks. Take the 2017 MTV Movie Awards. She took a little risk with an uber-short menswear-inspired fringed tuxedo jacket that she donned as a dress.

Then, at the 2018 Vanity Fair Oscars Party, she sparkled in an Armani Privé red sequin gown that is one of her best looks to date!

[sendtonews type="float" key="lcjHjLqwrb-2839059-14453"]

More recently, the actress has been leaning toward black and white ensembles. At the 2019 Met Gala she rocked a Givenchy lace look, complete with a white caped mini dress and black thigh-high boots.

She stuck with the color theme at the 2021 Critics’ Choice Awards too, showing up in a Prabal Gurung power suit. The menswear-inspired getup featured a wide-leg black pant and white ruffle top.

In celebration of the star, we’re taking a look back at some of Gadot’s best red carpet fashion and beauty moments. Keep scrolling to see our favorite dresses, suits and glam!
