Who is Kermit Washington:

Kermit Washington is a famous Basketball Player. He was born on September 17, 1951 and his birthplace is Washington, DC. Kermit is also well known as, Power forward who is best remembered for an on-court fight against Rudy Tomjanovich, in which a punch he delivered nearly killed his rival.

Kermit is originated from United States. As a rookie with the Los Angeles Lakers, he admitted to often feeling intimidated by legendary teammate Jerry West.
