Who is Meek Mill dating?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, February 6, 2024

RAPPER Meek Mill has been romantically linked to fashion designer Milan Harris - who gave birth to their child in 2020.

Their son was born on May 6 - the musician's birthday.

Who is Meek Mill dating?

While Meek Mill and Milan Harris are close, it's not clear if they are a couple in 2021.

The rapper posted on social media during the summer of 2020 that the couple "came to [an] understanding" as will serve as "separate parents."

"Me and Milano decided to remain friends and build our child as separate parents ... we still have mad love for each other but we both came to a understanding," read the post, according to People.

"No fall out either just moving forward! Still love! This for protection so social media won't think we moving wrong."

Meek Mill, who has two other children, made a celebratory post when Harris gave birth.

"Milano dropped me off a king on my birthday!" he tweeted at the time.

In 2019 Harris denied rumors she was dating Mill, according to Us Weekly.

“To be honest, Meek was the first celebrity to ever wear my stuff," she said.

"In 2012 he wore one of my hoodies. And it was an $80 hoodie at the time and that’s literally when I started.

"Meek supports Milano and I support Meek. That’s my friend.”

What did Meek Mill say about Kobe Bryant?

In a new song Meek Mill appears to say, "If I ever lack I'm going out with my choppa, it be another Kobe," in a preview of the track, Don’t Worry (RIP Kobe).

The song comes just three weeks after Kobe's family, friends, and fans marked the first anniversary of his and daughter Gianna's tragic deaths.

Kobe and his daughter were killed alongside seven others in a helicopter crash in California in January of 2020.
