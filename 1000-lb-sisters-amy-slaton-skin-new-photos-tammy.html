1000-LB Sisters' Amy Slaton has shown off her natural skin in new filter-free photos during a concert.

Tammy, 36, and Amy, 35, went on a concert date together to watch rapper Kevin Gates perform. 

In the filtered Instagram collection, the 1000-Lb. Sisters alums cuddled and made silly faces at the camera.

The TLC stars stuck out their tongues and pouted their lips.

In one non-filtered photo, Amy ditched the makeup as she teased cleavage from her low-cut tank top. 

While posing barefaced, Tammy's sister wore accessories such as rings, earrings, and bracelets and flaunted her bright blue nail polish.

 On TikTok, the 35-year-old star shared another selfie and announced where she was heading off to.

HER WEIGHT LOSS JOURNEY

The TV star continues to display her slimmer face and the results of her weight loss journey.

When Amy first appeared on 1000-Lb. Sisters in 2020, she weighed over 400 pounds.

After having gastric bypass surgery in 2019, the mom of two dropped to around 275 pounds.

Her sibling Tammy also planned to lose weight after tipping the scales at 700 pounds.

Tammy lost 300 pounds during her battle against obesity.

"When Tammy was first in rehab, she needed to reach her goal weight of 550 pounds from around 700 pounds to be approved for her bariatric surgery," a friend of the reality star exclusively told The U.S. Sun.

Amy shares her two little ones, Gage, two, and Glenn, nine months, with her estranged husband, Michael Halterman, 40.

THE SPLIT

As The U.S. Sun first reported in February, the former couple quietly split after five years of marriage.

In March, Tammy's ex filed for dissolution of marriage with children against his former partner, according to Kentucky court documents exclusively obtained by The U.S. Sun.

At the beginning of May, the exes faced off in court, where the judge granted their wish to break their strict 500-feet-apart and no-contact rule to swap their vehicles.

A civil restraining order will be filed separately as the open divorce case continues.

Tammy previously dropped the domestic violence claims against Michael in court.

According to an eyewitness who reported to The U.S. Sun, the TV personality exited the courthouse with her half-sister Amanda Halterman, 42, who had been flailing her arms and appeared to be ranting.
