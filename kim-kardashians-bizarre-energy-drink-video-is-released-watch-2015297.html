Kim Kardashian's Bizarre Energy Drink Video Is Released: Watch!

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, March 21, 2024

Some really weird things happen inside Kim Kardashian‘s head! The pregnant reality TV star’s latest advertising campaign has launched, and the short film made to promote Hype Energy Drinks is totally bizarre!

The future mom-of-two shot the video prior to becoming pregnant for the second time, as Us Weekly reported previously. And while the photos from the ad campaign were eye-catching enough (with Kim striking some serious poses as an intergalactic princess, Audrey Hepburn, and Marie Antoinette) the finished short film is a whole other ball game!

In the trippy video, Kardashian, 34, (complete with Hepburn bangs and black turtleneck) goes for a ride on her bicycle, the front basket stuffed full of energy drinks. The curvaceous actress hits a bump in the road and bangs her head (and all her energy drinks) hard on the floor.
