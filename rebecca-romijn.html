Rebecca Romijn Biography

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, May 27, 2024

American actress Rebecca Romijn is mainly popular in the world for portraying the role of Mystique in the X-Men films. Rebecca is also a former fashion model, and she is also known for her appearance in the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue and Victoria's Secret.

Rebecca Romijn, Bio, Age, Parents

Rebecca Romijn was from a middle-class family born as Rebecca Alie Romijn in Berkeley, California, the United States of America to Elizabeth Kuizenga and Jaap Romijn. She is an American and is of Dutch and English descent.

Love Affairs, Boyfriends, Husband, Divorce

In 1994, when Romijn was modeling at Victoria's Secret fashion show, she met John Stamos as he was attending the show as a guest.

A few months later they began their love affairs and on Christmas Eve of 1997, news about their engagement became aired. They finally got married on the 19th of September 1998. As a married couple, they never welcomed a child together and they got divorced 1st of March 2005.

The feud between Romijn and Stamos protruded in 2004 and they announced their separation in April 2004 which was also the same time she began dating Jerry O'Connell.

After her divorce with Stamos became finalized in 2005, she got engaged with Jerry O'Connell in September of the same month and she married him on the 14th of July 2007. The couple is often seen spending holidays together. They are still together and happy as loving spouses and they have welcomed a twin at the end of 2008.

Career: Movies and TV shows

While Romijn was at the University of California, she began her modeling career from there in 1991. She then started modeling for Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue, Victoria's Secret, and many more.

After gaining name and fame in the modeling industry, she entered into the acting world by appearing in one episode of the television series Friend, and her big break as an actress came after she appeared in the movie, X-Men in 2000.

What is her net worth?

As an actress, her salary is high and her net worth is rumored to be around $25 million.
