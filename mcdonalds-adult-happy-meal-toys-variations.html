McDonald's adult Happy Meal toys: What are the variations?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, March 3, 2024

MCDONALD'S announced in late 2022 that they would be releasing adult Happy Meals for a limited time.

Along with the new menu options, McDonald's also announced a collaboration with streetwear brand Cactus Plant Flea Market, which helped design the new adult-themed toys.

What are the McDonald's adult Happy Meal toys variations?

The Happy Meal is a popular choice on McDonald's menus for younger children that first launched in 1979.

Along with the bright red box with a yellow smiley face, the meal often comes with a toy based on a popular movie or TV show.

So when McDonald's announced their adult Happy Meals, consumers were not shocked when they also released adult-themed toys to go along with them.

With each adult Happy Meal, fast-food lovers nationwide will have the opportunity to get one of four limited edition toys based on classic McDonald's characters.

"Everyone remembers their first Happy Meal® as a kid... and the can’t-sit-still feeling as you dug in to see what was inside," the company said in a press release, via Mashable.

"That little red box could turn a regular Tuesday into the best. day. ever. And now, we’re reimagining that experience in a whole new way — this time, for adults."

The toys include distorted versions of Grimace, Hamburglar, and Birdie, which were iconic characters often featured in McDonald's commercials.

There will also be a Cactus Buddy toy available.

How long will adult Happy Meals be available?

Consumers have the option of ordering either a Big Mac or a 10-piece nugget meal that comes with fries, a toy, and a drink.

The adult Happy Meal launched on October 3, 2022, and will only be available through October 30.

At this time, it is unclear if the adult Happy Meals will return after October 30.

What have people said about McDonald's adult Happy Meal?

Shortly after the nationwide launch, several consumers took to Twitter to comment on the new meals.

One user said: "I’ve done a lot of things I’m ashamed of but ordering a McDonald’s Adult Happy Meal is not one of them."
