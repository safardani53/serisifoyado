Shopper's rant about 'Spicy Meat Feast' pizza from Spar with one tiny jalapeno goes viral - and earn

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, September 14, 2024

A SHOPPER who moaned about the lack of chillies on her 'spicy' Spar pizza earned a free takeaway after her rant went viral.

Ursula Jordan took to Facebook to vent her anger after opening her meat feast to find just one solitary slice of jalapeno on it.

She had bought it from her local spar in Sleaford, Lincs as she was in “the mood for some spice”.

She posted a photo of it and wrote: "Is this some kind of sick joke, Spar?

"Thinking if I maybe bought it on April Fools or something."

The post was soon shared hundreds of times.

It also caught the attention of a local takeaway pizza joint who offered to send her a replacement.

James Broderick from Little Italy, commented: "Ursula, call Little Italy - tell them James sent you and I'll have a free 12" pizza with your choice of toppings sent to your door for free."

Ursula said: "I wasn't expecting a response like that but it was very much appreciated."
