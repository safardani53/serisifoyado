Jaden Smith abruptly deletes concerning posts after sparking fears with strange behavior and photos

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, September 27, 2024

JADEN Smith has sparked fear after abruptly deleting concerning social media posts and displaying strange behavior.

Earlier this week the 25-year-old rapper deleted all of his Instagram posts, leaving just some album cover art from 2021.

He then uploaded just two plain white squares, leaving fans concerned for his well-being.

The Karate Kid actor's followers have shared their concern in the comments, with one asking: "What are you doing sweetheart?"

Another wanted to know: "Jaden what is this?"

Some fans predicted the absence of posts could be leading up to an announcement from the artist.

One commented, "It might be an album cover he's making," and another added: "Uh oh, new era coming soon."

"New album coming?" a third user questioned.

After this speculation by fans, the star deleted both of the white squares from his profile.

On Tuesday, Jaden posted nine black and white model shots promoting his collaboration with the sportswear brand New Balance.

The star posted a photo of himself facing diagonally to the camera with a leather jacket and no pants on, wearing a pair of white trainers.

This announcement comes after Jaden displayed concerning behavior.

The Pursuit of Happyness star was spotted dancing with a chair above his head during a wild night out.

Days later, he was caught singing in the street with a group of friends.

The Karate Kid actor belted out the lyrics to songs from High School Musical while carrying a glass of champagne in his hand.

Jaden was also spotted trying to keep a low profile while smoking a suspicious-looking cigarette.

SECRET SPLIT

On Wednesday it was also announced that Jaden's parents, Will and Jada Pinkett Smith had been secretly separated for seven years.

The actress, 52, admitted they have been trying to "figure out" what the future of their marriage looks like.

Jada and Will, 55, married in Baltimore on New Year’s Eve in 1997, while she was pregnant with their son Jaden, now 25.

Reflecting on their relationship, she told People: “We’re still figuring it out. We’ve been doing some really heavy-duty work together.

"We just got deep love for each other and we are going to figure out what that looks like for us.”

As for their kids, the Red Table Talk star said: “My children, they’re little gurus. They’ve taught me a deep sense of self-acceptance.”

WILLOW'S THOUGHTS

Jaden's sister Willow Smith has also posted worrying posts on Instagram.

Following the public news of her parent's split, she shared a text card that showed a definition of the term resentment.

"Resentment is literally the experience of feeling something over and over again (re-sentiment)," the description said.
