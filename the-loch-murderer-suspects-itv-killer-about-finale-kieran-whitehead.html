Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, August 16, 2024

VIEWERS of The Loch desperately tried to figure out who was behind the gruesome murder of Niall Swift.

The murderer of Loch Ness turned out to be Kieran Whitehead (Jack Bannon) who was actually pretending to be his brother Jordan, who had been in a coma since the start of the series. Here's everything you need to know about the show that kept you guessing right to the end.

Who was the killer?

The killer was Kieran Whitehead (Jack Bannon) who was actually pretending to be his brother Jordan who had been in a coma since start of the series.

It turns out that the body in the lake was that of his violent father and Kieran had killed him over abuse he received as a child.

Kieran revealed the truth to his friend Evie when he realised that his father's body had been identified and that it would end up coming back on him.

Who was killed on The Loch?

Niall Swift

The main victim on the show is Niall Swift, a gay piano teacher whose body was found at the bottom of Carn Mohr Mountain.

Bizarrely, part of his brain had been removed through his nose while he was alive - and his is not the only body involved in the show's overall mystery.

School kids later found a human heart not far from the crime scene.

Johnjo Patterson

Johnjo was a local teenager who disappeared later on  in the series.

After a frantic search, the kid was tragically found by the police in the back of a black van.

The heartbreaking scenes saw Johnjo's mother left completely distraught by the discovery.

The Man on the Beach

Viewers saw the corpse of an old man held down by a curling stone in the loch, with a hole showing where his heart had been removed.

Up until the end of episode five, we were the only ones to know the body was even there - and we still don't know who he is.

However, at the end of the penultimate episode, a group of Loch-Ness hunting tourists saw the body on the tour boat cameras.

Weighed down to remain under water, we are achingly close to discovering who is responsible for the killings.

College students

When Dessie Toner (Conor McCarry) took a hunting rifle on a college trip and killed the bully Baxter, the police were convinced he was responsible for all the murders.

Dessie also lost his life in the tragedy.
