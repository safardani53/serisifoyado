Jennifer Garner, John Millers Relationship: Everything We Know

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, May 11, 2024

Jennifer Garner’s romance with John Miller is going strong after a brief break.

The pair began dating in 2018, the same year Garner finalized her divorce from ex-husband Ben Affleck. The actors — who share kids Violet, Seraphina and Samuel — ended their 10-year marriage in 2015.

Miller, meanwhile, was still legally married to ex-wife Caroline Campbell when he and Garner started seeing each other. The former couple, who share two kids, split in June 2011 and got back together in February 2012. They filed divorce papers again in October 2014.

After two years together, Garner and Miller amicably split in August 2020, but Us Weekly confirmed in May 2021 that they had rekindled their romance. “Jen and John are back on,” a source shared at the time. “It started up a few weeks ago.”
