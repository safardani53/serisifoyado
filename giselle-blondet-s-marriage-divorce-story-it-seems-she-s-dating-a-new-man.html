Giselle Blondet marriages, divorce and children.

Giselle first got married back in 1982 to her first husband, Luis Iglesias. They also have a daughter named Andrea together. However, the marriage did not last very long as the pair got a divorce in 1984 just 2-years after their marriage.

The pair legally separated a year later in 1987. 

Her third marriage was with Harold Trucco, an Argentine producer in 1989. Together, the couple had their first child, Blondet's second, Harold Emmanuel. Following the birth of their first child, they welcomed their second child, a daughter named Gabriela.
