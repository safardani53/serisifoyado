Chelsea Handler: &quot;I'm Going to Stop Making Fun of Tori Spelling&quot;

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, June 11, 2024

Chelsea Handler is vowing to cut down on the Tori Spelling cracks on her hit E! show Chelsea Lately.

"Tori's sweet," Handler told Usmagazine.com Saturday at the Chrysalis Butterfly Ball in L.A. She hasn't always felt that way. Handler has long poked fun of Spelling, everything from the actress' obsession with gay men to her thin frame ("I'm a little full right now. I just ate Tori Spelling," she recently quipped). Spelling even jokingly thanked Handler at Bravo's recent A-List awards "for finding ways to say I'm ugly and stupid, week after week."
