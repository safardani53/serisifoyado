Primark is selling FLAMINGO slippers... and shoppers are going wild for them

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, July 25, 2024

PRIMARK is selling a pair of giant flamingo slippers - and shoppers think they're “amazing”.

The novelty footwear is candyfloss pink, features the bird’s head at the front and glittery wings on each side.

View this post on Instagram

A post shared by Primark (@primark)

But, they are currently only available in Ireland and are priced at 10 euros.

The Sun Online has contacted Primark to see if the cosy animal slippers will be flocking to the UK anytime soon.

A photograph of the flamingos racked up more than 95,000 likes on Instagram.

“Ohh my god! There are no words!” commented one excited customer, while another added: “Oh my god I am getting these.”
