How many kids does Lil Baby have?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, June 1, 2024

LIL BABY has earned several accolades since his breakthrough in the music industry following the release of his mixtape in 2017.

He has had two children with two different women during his career.

Who is Lil Baby?

Born on December 3, 1994, in Atlanta, Georgia, Lil Baby is a well-known rapper who rose to fame following the release of his mixtape, Perfect Timing, in 2017.

Lil Baby, whose real name is Dominique Armani Jones, created his first studio album, Harder Than Ever, in 2018.

Since then he has released other mixtapes and albums, including his second studio album, My Turn, in 2020.

He has also collaborated with other veteran musicians such as Nicki Minaj, Kanye West, The Weeknd, and Lil Durk.

As a result of his success, he has taken home a Grammy Award, MTV Video Music Award, and two BET Awards.

At the Apple Music 2020 Awards, he was awarded the biggest all-genre Artist of the Year.

Lil Baby released his third album, It's Only Me, in 2022. The track list featured other popular rappers, such as Young Thug, Future, and Pooh Shiesty.

According to Celebrity Net Worth, Lil Baby has an estimated net worth of approximately $8million, as of June 2023.

His main source of wealth is said to be his music career.

He also earns income from brand endorsements and sponsorships.

How many kids does Lil Baby have?

Lil Baby has two children as of 2023.

The rapper had his first son, Jason, with ex-girlfriend, Ayesha Howard, in 2015.

Four years later, he welcomed his second son, Loyal, with his ex-girlfriend, Jayda Cheaves, on February 18, 2019.

Is Lil Baby married?

Lil Baby is currently not married.

However, his last relationship was with Jayda Cheaves.

They started dating in 2016, however, the pair broke up in March 2021.

Jayda is a serial entrepreneur who is known as an Instagram model and YouTube sensation.

Ayesha Howard, who is known on social media as Little Ms. Golden, is the mother of Lil Baby's oldest child, Jason. The pair dated from 2010 to 2015.
