Nick Jonas Asked About Sex With Kate Hudson: 'Unbelievable Connection'

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, February 21, 2024

Enough with the chitchat: “Are you having sex with Kate Hudson?” Well, that’s exactly what Nick Jonas was asked during an interview with Complex magazine. The former Disney star played coy, but eventually opened up about his relationship with the actress.

“Umm. You know, it’s interesting. You’re allowed to ask me whatever the f–k you want and I’ll answer it, or not,” Jonas, 23, said, laughing.

Complex’s Kerensa Cadenas replied: “You’ll answer it in whatever ways it fits.”

And that he did.

“Kate’s incredible. We had an unbelievable connection as two humans who just admire things about each other, and see something in each other that’s beautiful,” Jonas continued. “Out of my best effort to respect her and her privacy, I’m not going to say if we had sex or not. But we did have a beautiful connection. Even now I have so much admiration and respect. She’s amazing.”

Jonas, who has been asked about the budding romance before, didn’t mind the question. “It doesn’t shock me or surprise me. I don’t get pissed off about it,” Jonas told Complex. “I’ve come to accept it. With that in particular. It’s a bizarre matchup to a lot of people.”

As Us Weekly exclusively revealed, Jonas and Hudson, 36, hooked up late last year after being introduced by Hudson’s brother, Oliver Hudson. (Oliver and Jonas costar on the Fox horror comedy Scream Queens.) The pair first sparked dating rumors when they were photographed in Walt Disney World in Orlando in September 2015. Two months later, the Kung Fu Panda 3 star even bared her bum for a sexy bathtub Instagram photo to make the “Jealous” singer, um, jealous.

“They are both just having fun,” an insider told Us at the time. “She’s just seeing this as a fun hooking thing right now.”
