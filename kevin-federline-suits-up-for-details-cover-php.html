Kevin Federline suits up for ‘Details’ cover

» More Celebs


The best day ever, ladies and gents, has arrived. Take one part house-husband, one part Queerest Eye makeover and one part Daniel Peres still refuting his magazine is gayer than AXM, and what do you get? Mrs. Britney Spears on the cover of Details – plus a full-out photo spread and feature (that’s the one with words and paragraphs).

Britney was on-hand to offer her stylistic advice (we weren’t sure if that was for serious or to remind the actual wardrobe consultants to do the exact opposite of what she said) as her back-it up dancer husband Kevin Federline posed for photog Steven Klein. And Kevin wore suits, like the single-breasted kind! Like, the one you might have seen him wearing during his wedding but nowhere else ever.

But fuck the photo spread. We’re dying to see Peres drool all over Federline in the issue’s editor’s letter.
