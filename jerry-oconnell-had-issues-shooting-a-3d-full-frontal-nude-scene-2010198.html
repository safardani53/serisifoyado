Jerry O'Connell Had &quot;Issues&quot; Shooting a 3D Full-Frontal Nude Scene

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, September 25, 2024

— Jerry O'Connell is taking it all off in his latest flick, Piranha 3D — but he promises it isn't as sexy as it sounds. "I show frontal. I play a soft core pornography mogul who videotapes girls on Spring Break, and that's what this is about," the actor, 36, said on Jimmy Kimmel Live. "My character, I don't want to give too much away, but it is in 3D and there may be some issues with the piranha and my member." [Huffington Post]

— Beyonce Knowles isn't exactly a domestic diva. In the September issue of Self, the 28-year-old singer admits that she has a hard time tidying her posh pad with hubby Jay-Z. "I promise I'm working on this, but I could be less messy at home and keep the clothes in my closet and off the floor," she says. [Pop Eater]

— Latin superstar Ricky Martin — who announced he is gay earlier this year — has just unveiled the cover art for his memoir, Me, due in stores on November 2. "From the moment I wrote the first phrase, I was sure the book was the tool that was going to help me free myself from things I was carrying within me for a long time," the singer, 38, has said. "Things that were too heavy for me to keep inside. Writing this account of my life, I got very close to my truth. And this is something worth celebrating." [Just Jared]

— Man Men's Christina Hendricks has graced numerous magazine covers, but the 35-year-old actress' mug most recently appeared in an unusual place — online handmade marketplace Etsy.com! The curvy redhead poses in a series of photos modeling scarves for her friend Tamara Mello (best known for her role on the short-lived series Popular). [MSNBC]
