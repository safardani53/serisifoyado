[PICS] Rihannas Ponytail In Barbados See Her Waist-Length Hairstyle Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 12, 2024

Rihanna, 28, just teamed up with Prince Harry, 32, to raise awareness about HIV/AIDS, in recognition of World AIDS Day on Dec. 1. The Barbadian singer wore high-waisted, wide-leg white and pinstripe pants with a white shirt tucked in for an effortless look. Joining Harry to be tested for HIV as part of the Barbados National HIV/AIDS Commission’s “Man Aware” event, Rihanna’s whole look was casual but chic.

To complete her look, Rihanna opted for a simple beauty look, probably in an effort to work with the humid weather. Keeping the long extensions she’s been sporting as of late, Rihanna’s hair was left curly before it was pulled back into a high pony. To create a sleek finish, Rihanna’s hair was slicked back at the crown of her head with the help of a styling wax like Fatboy Tough Guy Water Wax, $21.

For her makeup, Rihanna sported a more fresh-faced look with clean skin and a shiny neutral lip. To add some definition to her eyes, Rihanna used soft brown eyeshadow shades with a touch of mascara as the finishing touch.

Rihanna has had a busy week, being honored in NYC at the Footwear News Achievement Awards just days before she headed to Barbados. Accessorizing her ponytail for that event with a baseball cap, Rihanna was the first woman ever to receive the Shoe of the Year award for her Fenty Puma Creepers.
