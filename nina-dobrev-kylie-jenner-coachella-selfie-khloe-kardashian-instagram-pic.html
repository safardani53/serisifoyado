Nina Dobrev & Kylie Jenner’s Coachella Selfie With Khloe Kardashian: BFFs?

Nina, Kylie, Khloe and another friend all squeezed together for a selfie on April 19, and everyone looks like they’re having a blast hanging out together!

“I love this sh–!” Kylie captioned her Instagram pic. We love it, too, Kylie, we really do! Next time we’re hoping to see Kendall Jenner, 19, squeeze in there too. (#BFFdreams.)

While Nina enjoyed the festival with her good pals Joshua Jackson, 36, and Diane Kruger, 38, after wrapping up her time on The Vampire Diaries, Kylie and Khloe were partying it up all weekend with Kendall and Tyga, 25.

Kylie and Kendall supported the rapper while he took the stage at Coachella on April 17, and they all hung out together at Khloe’s huge Coachella party. Kylie has been bringing her style A-game to Coachella this weekend, from sporting bright pink hair, to streaking her natural dark hair white!

Nina Dobrev At Coachella After ‘TVD’ Wraps Up

Nina appears to be in great spirits now that she’s wrapped up TVD and said a tearful goodbye to her cast mates, but also leaving a funny goodbye video with Michael Trevino, 30.

While it pains us she will no longer be apart of the show, it seems that she’s ready to start a new chapter of her life — perhaps with Kylie and Khloe as her BFFs!

HollywoodLifers, what do you think of this epic meet-up? Do you want Nina, Kylie and Khloe to become friends? Let us know!
