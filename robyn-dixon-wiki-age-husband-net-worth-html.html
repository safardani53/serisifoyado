Robyn Dixon Wiki: Rekindled Romance With Ex-Husband At Age 39 &amp; Net Worth Details

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 5, 2024

American television personality and event manager Robyn Dixon is not worried about her unsuccessful marriage. She got her ex-husband to hold her hand in the ups and downs of her life.

The Baltimore native is best known after being featured on the Bravo TV's reality series The Real Housewives of Potomac. Robyn has opened a lip line product Robyn Dixon Collection. She served as the public relations officer and the special events boutique TAA PR located in Washington, D.C.

Here are some riveting wikis about Robyn Dixon, ranging from her age to her married life with husband, Juan Dixon, 

1. Robyn Dixon's Wiki-Like-Bio

Robyn born on 31 March 1979, is the University of Maryland graduate who received a degree in business marketing. According to the wiki, she was a star college basketball player while studying at the University.

2. It All Began For Robyn & Juan Dixon At Highschool!

During the time in university, she met her husband Juan Dixon who was basketball star rival Maryland high school. 

The duo started dating together in 1996 and became high school sweethearts. And while vacationing at the Miami beach, Juan proposed her writing "Will you marry me?" on the sands of the beach. Finally, they tied the knot in 2005 at Lafayette Center in Washington, D.C. in front of their family and friends.

Robyn and Juan shares two sons, nine years old Corey and eight years old Carter.

3. Divorced But Not Divorced

However, the couple was divorced in 2012 after seven years of marital relationship. But they are together fighting for the financial crisis even after their split.

In 2013, Robyn and Juan filed for chapter 7 bankruptcy due to which their financial troubles became an important issue on the Housewives series. Robyn has opened up about the problem created by the loss of money in the series.

After three years of filing bankruptcy, their 3,800-square foot house was foreclosed, which they bought for $725,000 in 2002.

The couple has resettled their fortune with the earnings they derived from the series. As of now, Robyn's net worth has reached $3 Million. Similarly, Juan is a current head coach for men's basketball team in Coppin State University in Baltimore, who has the net worth of $6 Million.
