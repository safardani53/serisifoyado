Buffalo Wild Wings brings back discontinued menu item and announces new option for NFL season

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, September 24, 2024

BUFFALO Wild Wings has added two exciting options to the menu, including a returning favorite.

The sports bar is gearing up for NFL season with some enticing flavors.

Buffalo Wild Wings has added two new flavors to the menu.

These options can be enjoyed on classic wings or boneless wings, sandwiches, and burgers.

Hot barbecue sauce is back - offering classic flavor with a spicy hit.

New for 2023 is Bulleit Bourbon barbecue sauce.

Read about more fast food

Made in collaboration with Bulleit Bourbon, the smoky-sweet sauce is infused with the brand’s Kentucky straight bourbon whiskey.

The result is a rich, complex barbecue flavor.

Buffalo Wild Wings decided it was time to introduce the new flavors in time for the NFL season, which kicks off September 7.

But you can find both new sauces on the menu as of August 16.

Buffalo Wild Wings competitor Wingstop also recently introduced a new flavor option.

The restaurant chain unveiled a new chicken wing flavor last month, designed in collaboration with Latto.

The Big Energy singer has teamed up with Wingstop to create Lemon Herb Remix, available to order now.

This dry rub packs zesty lemon, aromatic herbs, and savory garlic.

Wingstop has even launched a new combo meal with the hip-hop star, featuring 21 boneless wings, two dips, and one large fries.

“It's so good that you'll be fighting over the last wing, just like my sister Brook and I do,” said Latto in a press release.

Other fast food chains have also introduced some exciting new menu options as of late.

Taco Bell has reintroduced the beefy crunch burrito following a fan vote to bring it back.
