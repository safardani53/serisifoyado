Justin Bieber Throws It Back, Posts Beach Photo with Selena Gomez

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 22, 2024

Justin Bieber has given fans another reason to believe he’s back together with Selena Gomez.

Celebrating Throwback Thursday an evening early, the singer posted an image to his Instagram account on Wednesday night of him and Selena on the beach with Justin’s two young siblings.

It appears to be a photo from a 2012 trip the couple took to Malibu:

Justin and Selena haven’t been too shy about their reconciliation.

 

They held hands at Coachella; they met up for a late-night rendezvous in Miami this month; they may have made a sex tape (EEK!).

Meanwhile, Bieber has gone back to recording music and seemed apologetic on Twitter over some of his recent actions.

Coincidence? Many have attributed Justin’s terrible 2013 to the fact that he split with Gomez immediately prior to that year kicking off.

This latest Instagram photo is simply further proof that the twosome is doing well once again. We’re very glad to see it.
