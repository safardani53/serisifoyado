TEEN Mom's Cory Wharton revealed he had quit MTV's The Challenge because his daughter Mila, 1, said she could "barely recognize" him when he came back home. 

Cory, 30, explained his reasoning behind his exit from MTV's reality competition show on Instagram. 

The Teen Mom OG star shared a sweet snapshot of himself with his daughter Ryder, 4.

In the snap, the two were blowing air at the dandelion that she had picked up.

When he asked what she wished for, Ryder said: "I hope you don’t go back on The Challenge."

Cory captioned the post: "I knew that I needed to take a break from the show." 

While referring to Mila, he said: "If my little four-year-old is sitting there telling me: 'Daddy, I don’t want you to go and my other one-year-old could barely even recognize me when I came home from double agents, that’s a sign."

The MTV star realized he needed to create memories "with my family and my daughters," and not just for the show.

ASHLEY'S EXIT

“Because of that, Ashley is no longer able to stay in the game.

"She has been deactivated. She is no longer able to continue in this game for the rest of the season.”

MTV has made no further comment about her departure.

It is still unclear which rule or rules she had broken.
