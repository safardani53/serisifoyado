Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, February 27, 2024

TEEN Mom fans accused Kailyn Lowry of “creeping” on Briana DeJesus’ Instagram, years after the two had a nasty feud.

Kailyn, 29, and Briana, 26, first began feuding after Bri started dating Javi Marroquin, Kailyn's ex-husband and baby daddy.

The two have been shading each other on and off for years, but they’ve mostly kept their distance over the last year.

Now, fans on Reddit spotted mother-of-four Kailyn possibly “creeping” around on Bri’s old Instagram posts.

A user recently noticed a like had been made on Briana’s March 14 post of her two daughters, Nova and Stella, by Kailyn’s podcast account.

Though the Coffee Convos Podcast is also co-hosted by Lindsie Chrisley, many fans are speculating Kail was using it to look around on Bri’s page and accidentally liked one of her photos.

After seeing the Instagram like pointed out to them, one Redditer called Kailyn “a big messy b**ch,” while others said the whole thing gave them “secondhand embarrassment” and that Kailyn’s “pathetic!”

One Teen Mom fan suggested to Kailyn: “If that happened to me, I'd just follow. It's easier to claim growth & maturity if you like & follow. 

“She doesn't follow so now we all know she was creeping lol unless it popped up on her explore page.”

Coffee Convos Podcast only follows three accounts on Instagram, Kailyn’s, Lindsie’s, and Kail’s Pothead Haircare brand, which fans noted means Bri’s page wouldn’t have come up on the regular feed.

Kailyn shares her 7-year-old son Lincoln with Javi, and the two were married from 2012 to 2017.

Javi and Briana started dating shortly after but split in 2018.

Aside from Kailyn “creeping” on Briana’s Instagram and possibly stirring up their old drama, both Teen Moms have a lot of their own personal dramas that they’re currently dealing with.

Briana has been feuding with her ex and baby daddy Devoin Austin, claiming he isn't emotionally or financially supporting her and their daughter Nova, 9, enough.

The back-and-forth got so heated that Devoin leaked nasty text messages between the two of them online and didn't crop out her number.

After her number was made available for all to see, Bri said she was flooded with rude texts and phone calls and was forced to change her number.
