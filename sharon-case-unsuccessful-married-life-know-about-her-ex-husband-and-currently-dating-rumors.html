Sharon Case's Married Life; Know About Her Ex-husband And Dating Rumors

Despite the years, Case stills look so young and vibrant, but she didn’t get hitched to any other man. However, Case dated singer Jimmy Wayne in October 2009.

It is said that the pair’s relationship lasted for a couple of years, but it seems like they have broken up at present as there is no trace of Wayne on Case’s social networking accounts. Moreover, she is often spotted with different men whom she terms as ‘friends or co-star’ on her Instagram posts. Let’s find out the truth:

Sharon Case married life with Sandy Corzine

Case married Sandy Corzine in Mexico on 30th April 2007. The pair dated for more than two years before taking the big step in their life. Corzine was a successful businessman.

Despite it seemed like the pair’s married life was going well to all the fans, their relationship couldn’t last more than 3 years. The dissolve of her real-life marriage was similar to her reel-life.

It seems like the whole divorce process had deep impacts on Case as she only came out about her failed relationship after the year of its ending. The case never revealed the real reason behind her decision to separate from her then-husband.

Sharon Case Dated Jimmy Wayne

After Corzine, Case was said to be in a relationship with singer Jimmy Wayne according to However, the pair were never seen together.

Even if they dated for real, they might have already broken up now as Case’s Instagram is filled with her personal photos but Wayne is nowhere to be seen. But, she is often seen with other men such as her co-star Joshua Murrow.

It seems like Sharon is not in a relationship with Joshua as he is currently married to his wife Tobe Keeney. The couple tied the knot in 2001 and has been inseparable since then.
