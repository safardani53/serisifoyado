Jersey Shore's Angelina Pivarnick's buys $1.3M NJ mansion WITHOUT husband Chris Larangeira months af

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, May 8, 2024

JERSEY Shore star Angelina Pivarnick bought a $1.3million New Jersey mansion without her husband Chris Larangeira months after divorce rumors. 

The Sun can exclusively reveal Angelina, 35, bought a $1,275,000 Freehold, New Jersey mansion featuring four bedrooms and five bathrooms.

According to the Mortgage Deed obtained by The Sun, Angelina is the only buyer listed on the home despite owning a preview New Jersey abode with her husband.

The 5,609 square-foot home sits on a 1.19 acre lot.

The entryway boasts tile flooring and a butterfly staircase.

The first floor also features a living room with a marble fireplace, hardwood floors and a spacious office. 

The gourmet kitchen has a center island with seating, wood cabinets, stainless steel appliances and more amenities. 

The dining area leads to the sprawling and “professionally landscaped” backyard.

The “private oasis,” described by Realtor, features a heated in-ground pool and spa, KOI pond with duel waterfalls and more. 

As for the upstairs, the owner’s bedroom suite has a large sitting area and two walk-in closets, as well as a tiled bathroom with a tub. 

Each bedroom has its own bathroom attached with “ample closet space.”

'SO EXCITED!'

Angelina expressed her excitement on Monday when she did her final walkthrough and officially closed on the stunning home.

She said while showing off the upstairs: “So excited guys! Can’t wait for you to all see it! Two stairs look at that.”

The Jersey Shore star also posted a photo in her foyer with the gif: “New home owner.”

She then posted a photo of the outside of the home with the gifs “home sweet home” and “proud of myself.”

The MTV star then posted at dinner celebrating the house purchase with her friend Jovanna Del Plato, who starred on Pauly DelVecchio and Vinny Guadagnino’s A Double Shot at Love.

DONE WITH CHRIS?

Noticeably missing from the snaps and celebration is Angelina’s husband Chris, who is not listed on the deed. 

Angelina secretly filed for divorce from Chris, 42, in January a little over a year after their 2019 wedding, which was featured on Jersey Shore Family Vacation. 

The Sun previously reported Angelina’s divorce from Chris was dismissed after neither of them followed up.  

During the most recent season of Jersey Shore, a video began to circulate that made it look like Angelina was cheating on Chris.  

Angelina offered for him to watch it during an episode earlier this month, though Chris refused.  

She said in a confessional: “I’m an open book. I don’t like to hide secrets and hide s**t, so I decide to let my husband know I have these videos and he said he’s good with not seeing them."  

The MTV star continued: "I knew Chris wouldn’t care. He didn’t want to see them.  

“My husband is very well aware of what’s going on in my life and what’s going on in his life. There are no secrets, you know what I mean. This is our marriage."  

The conversation ended on a hopeful note, as Angelina told Chris: “At least we’re moving forward past this.”
