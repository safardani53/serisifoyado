Basic Comfort's Ultimate Crib Sheet Color &amp; Grow Bedding

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, February 1, 2024

I swear by Basic Comfort’s Ultimate Crib Sheet. This crib sheet is amazing because it’s the only cotton-topped waterproof sheet that snaps onto the bars of your child’s crib. Which makes it really easy to get it on and off, especially when you really need to put a fresh sheet on — at 4 am. What I didn’t know is that it comes in several different colors and has matching cute bedding. The top is 100% cotton, with the middle is polyester/rayon middle and the bottom is 100% vinyl.

The Ultimate Crib Sheet in Color comes in natural, cool blue, warm orange, purple with green dot, green with blue dot and rose. It’s a quick change sheet, a mattress pad, a waterproof pad and a super absorbent pad. In the two years since we’ve owned the original version, no liquids have ever leaked onto our mattress and it really does install and remove in seconds (without undoing bumpers, etc). Finally, I’ve been liberated from the boring white sheets that have covered our crib for the last 2 1/2 years!! Since Ben is still in the crib, he is currently testing out the Ultimate Crib Sheet in purple with green dots. It’s actually thicker than the regular Ultimate Crib Sheet (I recommend washing it a few times to get it softer).

I love Basic Comfort’s Grow Bedding Line – that coordinates with the Ultimate Crib Sheet in Color. Check out the Grow Sweet Pea 4 Piece Bedding Set. Perfect for a boy or girl, this darling set (just take the bumpers off) will also work for a toddler bed. I also loved the Bubbles & Fish set — in blue and bright green with white accents — so fun and whimsical. This would have been perfect for my toddler when he was in his previous room with the sea and sky them (which the baby has taken over and I intend to girlify a little).

The Grow Bedding Sets include:

  • Embroidered heirloom quilt; embroidered bumper set; tailored crib skirt & fitted crib sheet
  • All pieces are machine washable, and colorfast for longer wear

The Mod Flowers Set in lush purple, is adorable and definitely girly enough for Allegra. I love that it comes with a matching mobile (and even a sleep sack). Ben is currently using the the Grow Striped Lampshade with the natural wood lamp base. There are so many great choices from this company to grow your child’s nursery!
