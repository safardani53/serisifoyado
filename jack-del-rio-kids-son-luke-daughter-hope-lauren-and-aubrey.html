Son Luke &amp; Daughter Lauren, Hope And Aubrey

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, April 15, 2024

Jack Del Rio kids are in various fields, with his son, Luke, being the only one to carry on his football legacy. The NFL coach has raised four kids, three daughters, and a son, with his wife, Linda.

The footballing couple, Jack and Linda, have been married for 36 years. The two met for the first time after Jack was drafted by the New Orleans Saints in 1985. 

At first, Linda promised herself she wouldn’t fall for an NFL player, but destiny had other plans. The two tied the knot and went on to raise four kids. 

After the Commanders lost to the Chicago Bears in a humiliating fashion, 20-40, Jack Del Rio’s role as defensive coordinator was questioned by many. But Commanders star player Chase Young has defended Jack and said the loss was on players. 

Jack Del Rio Kids: Son Luke Del Rio Works With Him

Jack Del Rio’s kids include the Washington Commanders assistant coach Luke Del Rio. He joined Washington Commanders at the same time as his dad in 2020. 

A University of Florida graduate, Luke had first enrolled at the University of Alabama in 2013. He was redshirted and was considered the emergency quarterback. 

The following year, he joined Oregon State in time to participate in spring ball. But even there he only stayed for one season before finally arriving at Florida.

He stayed out of action in 2015 due to transfer rules and was granted extra years of eligibility by the NCAA. In 2016, Luke was named the starting quarterback but suffered multiple injuries.

The injury problem continued in 2017, and he started behind Feleipe Franks and Malik Zaire. Later that year, Luke forgone his sixth and final year of eligibility. That same year, he enrolled in a Master’s degree program at the University of Florida. 

He graduated with a Master of Science in Management, International Business in 2018. Before joining the footballing world, Luke joined Broadbridge as the Sales Support Specialist.

He worked at Broadbridge for four months before accepting the role of assistant football coach at Santa Margarita Catholic High School.

Luke later joined Washington Commanders as Offensive Quality Control. He is also their Quarterbacks Assistant Coach. 

The former University of Florida quarterback tied the knot in 2019 with McKenzie Del Rio (née Elizabeth). The two crossed paths while Luke was at Oregon State. 

Mckenzie graduated with a degree in Public Health from Oregon State in 2016 and previously worked as a Community Coordinator for Love Sweat And Fitness. The couple’s first child, Archie, was born on February 2, 2022. 

Jack Del Rio Three Daughters 

Jack Del Rio and his wife, Linda Del Rio, have been blessed with three daughters. And unlike their father and brother, the three girls have remained away from the athletic world. 

Hope Del Rio graduated with a Political Science and Government degree from Louisiana State University. She later enrolled at Loyola University New Orleans College of Law to earn her Juris Doctor in 2017. 

While pursuing her Juris Doctor, Hope worked as a Summer Associate at Kean Millers LLP and Blue Williams LLP. In 2017, she became a Federal Law Clerk for the former District Judge for the ED of Louisiana, Carl J Barbier. 

She is an attorney in Tampa, Florida, and previously served as an Associate Attorney for Adams and Reese and The Kullman Firm. 

Hope tied the knot with Joey Ryan in 2015, and the couple shifted to Florida in 2021. Joey is the CFO at TrustSwap and is a licensed CPA holder.

Aubrey And Lauren Del Rio Are The Other Two Daughters

Aubrey Del Rio graduated from the University of Alabama in 2016 with a Communications degree. She later became Assistant to the Chief of Protocol at Ordine di Malta. 

In 2017, Aubrey joined Patrons of the Arts in the Vatican Museums to serve as their Internal Organizations and Communications Manager.

Looking at her Instagram bio and Facebook handle, Aubrey seems to be into the Hindu religion. On social media, Aubrey is seen with a baby girl but hasn’t revealed her partner. 

Lauren Del Rio tied the knot with musician Stanton Moore at Four Mile Village, Florida, in 2018. The couple currently lives in New Orleans, Louisiana. 

The two were previously married and met while Lauren worked with WWOZ-FM. Luaren is available on Facebook and has given us a glance into her personal life with the drummer.
