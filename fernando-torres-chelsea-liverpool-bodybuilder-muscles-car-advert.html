FERNANDO TORRES looks bigger than ever after showing off his bulging muscles and bodybuilder frame in a car advert.

Torres, 39, retired from football in 2019 after starring at clubs like Liverpool and Chelsea.

The Spaniard was renowned for his silky touch and lightning speed.

But since taking up a touchline role as manager of Atletico Madrid’s U19s, Torres has stunned fans with his new-look physique.

The ace piled on pounds of muscle by lifting heavy weights and gorging on protein.

And Torres gave supporters another glimpse of his bulging biceps while advertising a new car.
