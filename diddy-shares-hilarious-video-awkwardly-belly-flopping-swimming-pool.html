Diddy shares hilarious video awkwardly belly-flopping into swimming pool after working on his div

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, May 18, 2024

DIDDY shared a hilarious video of him awkwardly belly-flopping into his swimming pool.

He said he was ‘working on his dive,' but it appears he may need some more practice.

Sean “Diddy” Combs shared the belly flop clip captioned: "Practicing my dive."

In his stories, she showed more of the dive, swimming over to the ledge and telling fans: "What's up I ain't spoke to y'all in a minute. You know what I'm saying. Had to finish this final phase of the metamorphosis. But check it out. It's Friday. You know whole world is still going crazing.

"You got to be still. Keep on working on ourselves. But if a great change don't come out of all of this that would be a shame. So to everybody out there that hasn't been using the time to go within, there's still time. There's still time."

Last month Diddy launched an anti-Trump party to represent the "needs of black people" because he said he believed the US is "on the verge of a race war."

The rapper announced the creation of "Our Black Party," which hopes to advance "a political agenda that addresses the needs of Black people" with just two weeks until the election.

"I'm launching one of the boldest things I've ever launched," he tweeted. "I'm launching a Black political party with some young Black elected officials and activists. It's called @OurBlackParty, it doesn't matter if you are Republican or Democrat."

Diddy said his number one priority is to "get [Donald] Trump out of office. HE HAS TO GO," adding "we can't allow this man to continue to try and DIVIDE US."

"The people that have the most responsibility and should be SCARED TO DEATH of this man are white people," he continued. "WE ARE ON THE VERGE OF A RACE WAR. [We] need to get [Joe] Biden in and hold him accountable. It would be irresponsible of me to have us hold our vote hostage," he said in another tweet.
