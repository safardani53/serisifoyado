Her Most Iconic Music Video Looks Of All Time Pics Hollywood Life

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, February 27, 2024

Britney Spears in her ‘…Baby One More Time’ music video. She exploded on to the music scene in 1998 with her iconic first single. She began her career from a young age, as a contestant on ‘Star Search’ in 1992 and at 10-years-old landed a role on Disney’s ‘Mickey Mouse Club’ along with fellow future pop stars Christina Aguilrea and Justin Timberlake. Britney broke big in 1998 with her debut single ‘Baby One More Time’ and it’s iconic music video featuring her in a schoolgirl’s outfit. She followed it up with her next album’s hit single ‘Oops I Did It Again’ in a red skin-tight jumpsuit. Her career would follow with iconic performances at the MTV Music Awards. After a break from music to have her two sons, she returned to a triumphant Las Vegas residency at the Palms beginning in 2013. It ended in 2017 and she announced a new residency at the MGM Grand, but had to bow out when her beloved father Jamie underwent a near-death health crisis.
