John Travolta Turns Up the Heat in Red Pants and Sunglasses

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, October 8, 2024

—John Travolta checked out the official sponsor of the Official Grammy Gift Lounge, MTG, during the 59th Grammy Awards at Staples Center in L.A.

—Khloé Kardashian rocked a House of CB mesh bodysuit during a lunch date with her sisters in L.A.

—Emily Ratajkowski wore a Diane von Furstenberg SS17 fur collar to the company’s fall 2017 dinner in N.Y.

—NeNe Leakes gushed, “I am a Beyoncé fan!” to hosts Frankie Grande and Rachel Smith on Amazon’s Style Code Live.

—Producers Marcello “Cool” Antonio Valenzano and Andre “Dre” Christopher Lyon of Cool & Dre attended the House of Stoli event hosted by Stoli Vodka in West Hollywood.

—Madonna and model Shaun Ross sat front row at the Philipp Plein runway show at NYFW in NYC.

—Amy Adams wore Toms Mackenzie Sunglasses in champagne honey brown gradient while leaving LAX.

—Demi Lovato wore a Milly Pre-Fall 2017 lace bustier and side cascade skirt while attending the Roc Nation Pre-Grammy Brunch in L.A.

—Hailey Baldwin wore sexy pieces from Meshki in an Instagram post.

—Gina Rodriguez wore Monique Lhuillier Spring 2017 while presenting in L.A.

—Nick Jonas wore David Yurman jewelry while performing and presenting at the Grammys in L.A.

—Dennis Quaid celebrated a pal’s birthday with Nutella pizza at Fresco da Franco in Montclair, N.J.

—Audrina Patridge promoted her line Prey Swim at Ryan Seacrest’s Purse Party at Bungalow Huntington Beach.

—Stevie Wonder and Republic Records CEO Monte Lipman celebrated with Freixenet Cava at Republic Records’ Grammy afterparty at Catch L.A.

—Santigold and Skylar Grey partnered with Absolut Lime to inspire meaningful conversations by wearing #RefreshTheTalk LED clutches designed by CuteCircuit on the red carpet at the Grammy Awards in L.A.

—Gretchen Mol, Carla Gugino and Connie Britton enjoyed casino games during the Bal du Paradis at the One&Only Ocean Club in the Bahamas. 

—Drew Taggart and Alex Pall of the Chainsmokers mixed cocktails at their pre-Grammy Turn Up at the L.A. home of Absolut Elyx CEO Jonas Tahlin.

—Kim Kardashian, Kendall Jenner and Bella Hadid chowed down on McDonald’s in between shows during NYFW in NYC.

—Orange Is the New Black’s Jessica Pimentel stayed at NYC’s the Gregory Hotel in Midtown’s Garment District, where she checked out the Caravan * TABII pop-up atelier and watched the making of eco-chic garments produced with zero waste during New York Fashion Week.

—Halsey performed at Delta’s annual Grammy celebration at Beauty & Essex in L.A.

—Danny Amendola and Olivia Culpo celebrated his Super Bowl win at a table in the VIP area at Marquee NY.

—Vampire Diaries’ Kat Graham got made up with Make Up for Ever products at Variety’s Portrait Studio at the Music Is Universal Lounge presented by Variety and Citi in L.A.

—Paris Hilton took selfies with design duo Phillipe and David, also known as the Blonds, at their fashion show at Skylight studios in NYC.

—Olivia Caridi and Izzy Goodkind played drinking games while enjoying the Bachelor viewing party at Brother Jimmy’s Murray Hill location in NYC.

—James Corden brought his parents to the Esquire Mavericks of Hollywood event presented by Hugo Boss at Sunset Tower in West Hollywood.

—Zayn Malik popped by the Billboard Power 100 celebration, presented by American Airlines, Citi, Nielsen Music and Hilton, at Cecconi’s in West Hollywood.

—Solange Knowles introduced honoree Erykah Badu at the Essence Black Women In Music event in L.A.

—Chord Overstreet posed for photos at Island Records’ pre-Grammys party at STK Los Angeles.

—Mario Lopez and Mayor Eric Garcetti hosted the 2nd Annual Run to Remember, which honors fallen police officers and first responders at The Grove in L.A.

—Joan Smalls wore POP Shoes LED Light Up Sneakers while out with Doutzen Kroes and Derek Blasberg in NYC.
