I'm NOT GAY with Soulja Boy!!

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, June 2, 2024

Rapper 50 Cent isn’t doing a good job making friends.  In October, 50 cent feuded with the cast of Empire and made himself look like a fool. And in August, 50 Cent flipped on Diddy in a serious of hilarious Instagram posts.

Now, the rapper is about to add another celebrity to his list of enemies: Vivica A. Fox.

On Sunday, November 8, Fox implied on Bravo’s Watch What Happens Live that her ex-boyfriend 50 Cent and rapper Soulja Boy are gay.

WWHL host Andy Cohen asked 51-year-old Fox about her response to 50 Cent suggesting that Empire’s rating dropped because of the show’s “gay stuff.”

Fox, who dated 50 in 2003, made an oblique comment implying 50 Cent is gay: "First of all, you know the pot called the kettle black is all I’m saying.”

WWHL co-guest Kandi Burruss of Real Housewives of Atlanta then added, "That was some tea! That was shade. That was major shade.”

Cohen then prodded for more salacious info asking Fox if she thought 50 was gay.  "No, he’s not," she said. "I mean, we had a great time. He just seems like he’s got something that’s not quite clear.”

The actress then reminded viewers of an old magazine cover that featured 50 and Soulja Boy: "He just looked like a booty snatcher on that one to me.”

It’s safe to assume that comment didn’t sit well with 50 or Soulja Boy.  In fact, it turned into an all-out social media war between the rappers and Empire actress.

Soulja Boy took to Instagram to confront Fox’s comments.  

He shared what some might consider to be an unflattering photo of Fox and captioned it, "Everybody hit up this cougar..she’s single & ready to mingle. 1-800-GrannyDesperateForAttention extension I’mWashedUp."

Meanwhile, 50 also had a few unfriendly comments for his ex-girlfriend.  He posted a screen grab of Soulja’s initial post and added, "Now she thinks I’m gay because I let her lick my a**.”

He continued, “Wait, I didn’t want her to, she forced me, my hands were tied. 50 shades of grey."

And just when you thought you knew too much about 50’s a**hole, he continued his rant.  50 posted a meme of Kevin Hart looking confused on stage.

The meme read, "Waiting on 50 cent to respond to Vivica Fox like…"  He added, "Man somebody should have told her I’m a Troll. I got my a–hole merit badge on now.”

Apparently, 50 Cent sat around and stewed in his anger for a while.  A few hours later, he began posting again:

“B*tch remember when you told me f— Halle Berry," he wrote.  "And you could have played all the roles she did better then her? And I just looked at you . . . like what the f–k kind [of pill] s–t did you take?LMAO.”

On Monday, Fox finally responded to Soulja Boy and 50 Cent’s Instagram stampede. Exactly! He 4gets I’ve got some stories in my vault too!! Try it…#Unbothered #LifeIsGood,” she posted.
