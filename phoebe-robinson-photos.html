Photos Of The Comedian &amp; Everythings Trash Star Hollywood Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 16, 2024

Phoebe Robinson is an actress, writer, and comedian who currently stars in the new series ‘Everything’s Trash.’ The show is inspired by her bestselling book ‘Everything’s Trash, But It’s Okay.’ The series premiered on July 13.

Prior to starring in ‘Everything’s Trash,’ Phoebe appeared on shows like ‘Broad City,’ ‘Late Night With Seth Meyers,’ and more. She was also a staff writer for ‘Girl Code’ and a season 3 consultant on ‘Broad City.’ She notably starred in the 2018 Netflix movie ‘Ibiza’ with Vanessa Bayer and Gillian Jacobs.

Her third book, ‘Please Don’t Sit on My Bed in Your Outside Clothes,’ was released in 2021. She had her own TV special, ‘Phoebe Robinson: Sorry, Harriet Tubman,’ which came out in 2021. Phoebe was featured in the 2020 Michelle Obama documentary ‘Becoming.’
