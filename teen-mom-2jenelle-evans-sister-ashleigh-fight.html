Jenelle Evans &amp; Sister Ashleigh FIGHTING Teen Moms War With Family Hollywood Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, February 21, 2024

“Jenelle is evil,” Ashleigh told Star magazine. “She’s always been jealous of me, but her latest stunt is her worst yet.”

“In early July, I was living with my husband, Nick [Wilson] in California,” she continued. “I finally hit rock bottom because of the way he was treating me.”

Ashleigh alleges that their mother Barbara Evans gave Jenelle $500 to fly out to California so the Teen Mom 2 star could help her sister pack and take care of her son Gabe, then they could all drive out to North Carolina together.

However, Ashleigh said her sister instead hung out, didn’t help, smoked pot, and left after three days, and pocketed all of the money for herself.

She also worried that Jenelle would turn over a video she secretly recorded of her yelling at her son, during a time Ashleigh admitted she was “stressed and overwhelmed,” so that Ashleigh would lose custody of her 6-month-old — and that’s exactly what Jenelle did.

On Aug. 22, Jenelle posted the video on YouTube and tweeted it out to her more than 450,000 followers with the message, “since my sister wants to sell stories about me…. lets just see psycho she is…”

In the video, Ashleigh is heard screaming, “I need sleep I need food! F–k kid, what is your f—ing problem? Gabriel, seriously man, I can’t deal with it much longer. I can’t hold you,” to her 6-month-old son Gabe.

Jenelle later wrote on Twitter that her mom Barbara texted her about a sex tape her sister allegedly has of her and a few other guys, which Jenelle denied exists.
