Who is GBBO 2022 contestant Maxy?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, February 23, 2024

THE Great British Bake Off returns for its thirteenth season along with a fresh batch of new bakers.

Joining the lineup is 29-year-old Maxy.

Who is Great British Bake Off 2022 contestant Maxy?

Maxy is an Architectural assistant from London.

she studied fine art and went on to achieve a Masters degree in Architecture, pausing her final qualifications to raise her two daughters, Tyra and Talia.

Her passion for baking began five years ago after she had her first daughter.

The 29-year-old was raised in Sweden and attributes her favourite flavours – cinnamon and saffron – to her Scandinavian upbringing.

What has Maxy said about joining Bake Off?

Speaking about appearing on the show, she said: "I have always been a Bake Off fan, but never did I expect to ever be in the tent baking, did not feel as if I was a good enough baker!

"Friends and family have urged me to apply but I never knew if they were actually being serious or not."

She added: "I decided to apply in the end, and if I am to be honest, never expected to receive a call back.

"When I received a call confirming my place I was in disbelief, was over the moon, I was pinching myself and constantly felt like I must have been dreaming. I was also a little confused, what? me? how? haha.

"But overall ecstatic, felt like I had won the lottery. I still cannot believe it."

Talking about her strengths and weaknesses, she added: "I would say that cakes are my strength and breads and pastries are my weaknesses."

Is Maxy on Instagram and TikTok?

Maxy is on Instagram with over 400 followers as of September 2022.

Over on her gram, you can expect to see stunningly crafted bakes which include scones, unicorn-inspired cupcakes, and bubble waffles.

Check her page out under the username @maxythebaker

Maxy does not appear to be on TikTok.

Who is in the Great British Bake Off cast?

The Great British Bake Off cast includes:

  • Maisam, 18, a sales assistant from Cornwall
  • Kevin, 33, a music teacher Kevin is from Lanarkshire
  • Sandro, 30, a nanny from London
  • Syabira, 32, a cardiovascular research associate from London
  • Dawn, 60, an IT manager from Bedfordshire
  • James, 25, a nuclear scientist from Cumbria
  • Janusz, 34, a personal assistant from East Sussex
  • Abdul, 29, an electronics engineer from London
  • Will, 45, a former charity director from London
  • Rebs, 23, a Masters student from County Antrim
  • Carol, 59, a supermarket cashier from Dorset

When is Great British Bake Off 2022 on TV?

The Great British Bake off kicks off with its new season on Channel 4 on Tuesday, September 13, at 8 pm.

There will then be new episodes that will air every Tuesday.

For the first episode, the contestants will be set one Signature, Technical and Showstopper bake by judges Prue Leith and Paul Hollywood.
