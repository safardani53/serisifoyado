Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, June 26, 2024

BREAKFAST Television's Sid Seixeiro has fumed over a beauty blunder that left him rubbing his face live on air.

On Tuesday's episode of BT, the daytime TV host had a few problems with starting his segment.

Sid sat next to Meredith Shaw to introduce a piece on the dropping temperatures in Canada and how they're decorating their homes for Christmas.

A festive song was playing over the introduction, to which Sid was heard humming along.

Meredith laughed: "You really don't know the words, do you?"

Sid replied: "I know the first song to every Christmas song, then you're on your own. Don't expect anything else out of me."

Meredith gestured to Sid's smart outfit as he sat up and joked: "The glam of Sid Seixeiro."

The screen soon moved back to the studio in Toronto, Sid excitedly said: "20 bucks to who knows the lyrics."

After just seconds, he pointed at the producers off-screen, and proclaimed: "Yeah, none of you win. No one wins."
