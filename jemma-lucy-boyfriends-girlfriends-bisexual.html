An inside look at Jemma Lucy's colourful relationship history - from Ashley Cole to Stephen Bear

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, February 6, 2024

THANKS to her appearances on Ex on the Beach and Celebrity Big Brother, Jemma Lucy's relationship history has unfolded in public for all to see.

Here's a brief history of the reality star studs Jemma has dated over the years - and what happened when they split...

Ashley Cole

While in Celebrity Big Brother, Jemma revealed to Chelsea fan Sam Thompson that she had bedded the footie ace shortly after his split with Cheryl.

She teased her housemate by revealing her Chelsea FC connections and joked: "I could ring my mate John Terry and ask for tickets but NOT go with you!"

"Fine," replied Sam, before quipping: "You've so slept with John Terry!"

Jemma however vehemently denied that and replied: "I haven't! The only Chelsea player I've ever slept with is Ashley Cole.

"I used to go out with him years ago when I was 20."

Stephen Bear

Jemma and Bear were inseparable for several months and even got matching tattoos together during their romance.

The pair kept tight-lipped about their budding relationship - until Jemma Lucy went on a sensational Twitter rant, in which she claimed Bear had cheated on her.

In May, pals admitted she had really fallen for Bear, and was even still in love with him.

Speaking to MTV, Bear revealed: “We had a good month together, and we even got matching tattoos so when we held hands it would become a heart.

“Now when I get married, I don’t think a girl would want another girl on my hand.”

She later branded him “for a good time, not a long time”.

Kirk Norcross

During her first appearance on Ex on the Beach in season three, Jemma charmed and eventually bedded Kirk Norcross.

However, she was left less than impressed and later told everyone about their x-rated night of fun.

"I did like Kirk but I wasn’t amazed by his sexual ability, especially considering how many girls he’d had sex with," she told Daily Star Online.

"He literally lasted three seconds and I did all the work.

"I was a bit blinded by liking him on the show but his d*** isn’t even that big."

Ashley Cain

Fuelled by booze, anger and jealousy, Ashley and Jemma leaped into bed together during the fifth season of Ex on the Beach – leaving Kayleigh Morris fuming.

The fall out of the sex session resulted in Jemma being kicked off of the show as the 29-year-old's scrap with her love rival got physical.

Kayleigh interrupted them in the middle of their tryst and refused to leave the room – causing the girls to scrap.

Jemma later quipped: "Sex with Ashley is f***ing dirty - and that's how I like it."

Gaz Beadle

She later defended their romp and said: "I was put in a situation in a penthouse with someone very attractive, I got drunk and I did get with Gaz.

"I probably shouldn't have.

"But I have a very high sex drive and, at end of the day, he put his d**k in me."

David Hawley

Hawley and Jemma clashed when they were reunited on Ex on the Beach – with Jemma frequently breaking down and revealing she even still loved him.

However on screen it didn’t work out, and after she confessed to sleeping with his best mate after an argument, she was left seething as he moved on to other women.

In 2017, he branded her “absolute venom” as she entered the Celebrity Big Brother house, and declared he wanted nothing more to do with her.

Scotty T

In a bid to start things with Hawley on a clean slate, she confessed to him that she had slept with Scotty T during a wild night out – leaving him furious.

However, Scotty denied he had ever bedded the tattooed beauty, and in response she gave a detailed account of their tryst in Weston-Super-Mare after a nightclub appearance together.

"We left, got in the taxi, him and her were kissing, then he'd kiss me, grabbing me around my waist, but then he'd keep stopping.”

"I will give him that, he knew he shouldn't be doing what he was,” she continued. “He's not completely dead inside, he's only half dead inside."

Marty McKenna

Marty McKenna was revealed to be one of Jemma's exes on Season 3 of Ex on the Beach - shortly before he joined the cast of Geordie Shore.

After washing up on shore, both enjoyed flings with other people in the house, but never reignited their own romance.

However, they enjoyed a brief reunion shortly after they got back from the villa, and they were seen tashing on in nightclubs less than a week later.

Since then, both have moved on to other people, with Marty's most notable relationship being with Geordie Shore co-star Chloe Ferry.

Laura-Alicia Summers

Laura and Jemma became the first lesbian couple to appear on Ex on the Beach – and was Jemma’s first major introduction into the limelight she’s in now.

The pair dated briefly for two months during their time on the show, and both identify as bisexual.

Feisty Jemma said their relationship fell apart at the time because they kept getting into arguments.

Chantelle Connelly

Jemma was connected to former Geordie Shore star Chantelle earlier this year – and soon they were pictures sharing loved-up holidays and snogging on nights out together.

However, after a few months their “romance” died out and they are now on less than positive terms.

Jury’s still out as to whether or not their relationship was real or one for the cameras – with differing accounts of the situation coming from both sides.

Either way, it was complicated.
