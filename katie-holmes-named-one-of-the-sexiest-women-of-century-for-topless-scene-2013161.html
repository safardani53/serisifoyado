Katie Holmes Named One of the Sexiest Women of Century for Topless Scene

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, April 7, 2024

Katie Holmes has made history! GQ has just named the former girl next door one of the sexiest women of the 21st century — due in large part to her topless scene in the 2000 supernatural murder mystery The Gift.

The magazine compares Holmes' role in director Sam Raimi's movie to her breakout role on The WB's Dawson's Creek. "She has the same Joey Potter tics: head tilts, shoulder shrugs up, lips chew down into a lopsided grin," writer Mark Lotto says of the TV role that made Holmes famous. "But then she takes off her top, revealing her un-Joey-Potter-like breasts — such exquisitely, precisely breast-like breasts, the breasts you would think of if someone said the word 'breasts.'"

Lotto jokes that Holmes' ex-husband, Tom Cruise, likely watches the actress' nude scene when he's feeling nostalgic. "And every time topless Katie taunts [Greg Kinnear] with a 'F-ck you,' Tom feels as if she's talking to him, and he thinks some terribly Greg Kinnearish things."
