Ravish Kumar Biography

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

He is a married man. He got married to his girlfriend Nayana Dasgupta. She is a history teacher at Lady Shri Ram College, Delhi University. They first met when Ravish Kumar was doing his MPhil. They fell in love and began their love affair and got married. They are very happy about being each other loving spouses. He loves his wife so much so there is no sign of getting the divorce. They have two children together.

He went to Loyola High School, Patna. Afterward, he moved to Delhi for his higher studies. He graduated from the Deshbandhu College University of Delhi. He earned his post-graduate degree from the Indian Institute of Mass Communication in Journalism. He began his career at NDTV and still he is associated with it. He earns a massive amount of salary and his net worth is not revealed yet.
