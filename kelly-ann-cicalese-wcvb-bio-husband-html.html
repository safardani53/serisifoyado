Kelly Ann Cicalese And Husband Are Parents To A Baby Boy Married Since 2015

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, April 23, 2024

Kelly Ann Cicalese and her husband Edward J Davilla are now proud parents of a son named Leonardo Edward.

The WCVB Storm Team 5's Weekend EyeOpener meteorologist welcomed her first baby on February 24, 2021. She confirmed the arrival of her baby through a Twitter post on 25th Febuary.

In the tweet, she posted a couple of pictures - the first picture was of the baby, as he was sleeping soundly, while in another picture, Cicalese and her husband were holding the baby.

Kelly Ann Cicalese and her husband Edward J Davilla with their newborn baby. (Photo: Twitter)

"My husband Edward & I are over the moon excited to announce the arrival of our son, Leonardo Edward," she captioned.

"Born 2 minutes before midnight, Leo...was determined to enter the world on February 24th."

Cicalese had previously shared the news of her pregnancy with the public in a very cryptic way in September 2020 via Twitter. 

In the post, dated September 6, she posted a video of herself doing the weather with a caption- "I have a special LABOR day forecast to share with you all." That caption would have just flown over people's heads had it not been for the baby and milk bottle emojis.

After her announcement, people were quick to decipher the coded message and congratulate her on her special day.

The metrologist has been married to her husband, Davilla, since October 11, 2015. She had met him in her native town of New Jersey at Rutgers University.

Kelly Ann Cicalese shares her wedding pictures on Facebook. (Photo: Kelly's Facebook)

The university is also the same place where the pair got married. Their wedding took place in a chapel on the main campus in New Brunswick.

Davilla is a Supervisor/Senior Customer Sales and Service Associate at Salem Five Bank.

Kelly Ann's Career: From WWOR TV To WCVB

Expressive and motivated broadcast meteorologist experienced in live anchoring, Cicalese started her career in broadcast meteorology in Cheyenne, Wyoming at CBS News. 

Before that, she was an intern at WWOR TV, where she worked at the Meteorology and Newsroom at My9 News. She later worked in meteorology and broadcasting at different organizations for a long time before finally joining her current workplace.

She worked at CBS News, NBC News, and WSAZ TV for around 15 years altogether before joining WCVB in October 2016.

Kelly Ann Cicalese forecasts weather of different places in the United States. (Photo: WCVB.com)

Cicalese, who has an Associate Degree in Mathematics, was also awarded the "Best Weather Broadcaster" title by the Wyoming Association of Broadcasters while she was at KGWN in Cheyenne, Wyoming.

How Much Salary Does Kelly Ann Cicalese Make?

In general, WCVB employees make an average of $51K per year. Kelly Ann Cicalese, of course, has experience of around 15 years in broadcasting meteorology and would earn an amount far more than the average.

Her years of experience have made her worthy of getting a high payroll in the reputed organization. What makes her a noteworthy weather forecaster is her ability to engage the audience by presenting information in a personable and professional manner.
