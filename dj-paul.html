Who is DJ Paul:

DJ Paul is a famous Rapper. He was born on October 13, 1977 and his birthplace is Memphis, TN. DJ is also well known as, Born Paul Beauregard, he is a rapper, producer, and member of the Academy Award Winning group Three 6 Mafia. In 2013 he reunited with four of the original member of Three 6 Mafia to form Da Mafia 6ix.

DJ is originated from United States. He collaborated with Justin Timberlake on the latter’s song “Chop Me Up.”
