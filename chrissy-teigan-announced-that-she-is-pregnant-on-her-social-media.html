Chrissy Teigan Announced That She Is Pregnant On Her Social Media

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, March 9, 2024

Chrissy Teigen and John Legend are expecting a rainbow baby, and fans are overjoyed. The 36-year-old revealed her pregnancy on Instagram on Wednesday, showing off her baby belly and an IVF injection mark. The mother seems to be a few months into her pregnancy based on the size of her bulge. The news went viral among her followers, who expressed their warmest wishes to the newlyweds. Teigan and Legend’s previous kid died after a miscarriage over two years ago.

In the news post, Chrissy Teigan shared an emotional letter with admirers.

Chrissy Teigan recently informed followers that although the “past few years have been a flurry of emotions,” the couple’s house is now again filled with “bliss.” She continued, saying:

“1 billion injections later (most recently in the leg, as you can see!) we have another on the way.”

The model said that she was nervous about informing her admirers. She elaborated:

“At every checkup, I tell myself, ‘OK, if it’s healthy today, I’ll announce,’ but then I hear a heartbeat and decide I’m still too anxious.”

She concluded the letter by expressing that everything was “wonderful,” and then she added:

“I’m filled with optimism and awe. Oh my goodness, it’s been a long time since I’ve done this!”

The story quickly went viral on social media, with over 2 million likes within 6 hours of being posted.

Fans are offering the newlyweds their warmest wishes.

Fans couldn’t stop swooning over the pair as the news emerged. Many of them wished the couple well and good health. Chrissy Teigen and John Legend miscarried in 2020. The couple tragically lost their third child at 20 weeks of the pregnancy term. Chrissy Teigen broke the news on Twitter, writing:

“We’re stunned and in the type of profound anguish you only read about, suffering we’ve never felt before. Despite many blood transfusions, we were unable to halt the bleeding and provide our son with the fluids he required. It just wasn’t enough.”

She posted a heartbreaking letter about her lost kid, explaining that they had begun to name him Jack. She stated:

“To darling Jack, I’m so sorry that the first few minutes of your existence were fraught with so many issues that we couldn’t provide you with the safe haven you need. We shall always cherish you.”

The model thanked her followers for their support through a difficult moment and posted:

“We are thankful for the life we have, for our great children Luna and Miles, and for all the fantastic things we have been fortunate to experience. However, not every day can be sunny. We shall lament and cry our eyes out on this darkest of days. But we will embrace and love each other even more, and we will get through it.”
