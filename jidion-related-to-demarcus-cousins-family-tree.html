Is Jidion Related To Demarcus Cousins? Family Tree Explored

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, February 6, 2024

DeMarcus Cousins and JiDion are not biologically related, but JiDion is a huge fan of the professional basketball player.

DeMarcus Amir Cousins is a professional basketball player for the Mets de Guaynabo of the Baloncesto Superior Nacional.

He debuted in the NBA with the Sacramento Kings after being drafted as the 5th overall pick in the 2010 draft.

Besides the Kings, Cousins played for the New Orleans Pelicans, Golden State Warriors, Houston Rockets, Los Angeles Clippers, Milwaukee Bucks, and Denver Nuggets of the NBA.

The professional basketball player was named to the NBA All-Star 4-time, All-NBA Second Team 2-time, and NBA All-Rookie First Team once.

DeMarcus Cousins’ Relation With JiDion

JiDion is also known as Demarcus Cousins III, which might be the reason why the fan thought they were related.

When asked about his name, JiDion said, “That’s just my grandpa, man. His name is Demarcus Cousins I, and my dad was Demarcus Cousins II, now I’m Demarcus Cousins III.”

He further added, “It’s not my fault that they’re not switching up the name. I might change it up and call my son Desmond or something, just to switch it up a little bit.”

JiDion is also a huge fan of the former Nuggets’ center. He thinks Boogie is the GOAT. The internet sensation loves how DeMarcus shows so much love.

He also mentioned that the two frequently exchange messages. Besides DeMarcus, JiDion has a good relationship with Ben Simmons and Mark Cuban.

JiDion met DeMarcus for the first time during a match between the Bucks and the Rockets. During that time, Cousins was playing for the Milwaukee team and clicked a picture with his “twin.”

JiDion’s Rise To Stardom

JiDion grew up in Houston, Texas, and was not loved by others as much as he is now.

During his school days, he struggled to make friends and found himself trying to align with different groups.

JiDion was not invited to parties and would find himself watching anime, reading books, and making funny videos. His main goal was to make somebody laugh with his videos.

With his funny videos, he had already started gaining popularity among the netizens. He rose to stardom on YouTube after he started streaming videos of him playing Call of Duty and Grand Theft Auto.

Later, he started to do reaction and commentary videos to viral internet content. His videos on YouTube are all aimed at giving happiness to the viewers and putting on a smile.

His journey to stardom was not smooth; at some point, he even thought of quitting YouTube. He was not generating as much revenue to make his living, and he started working at Lowe’s.

After encountering his fans at his workplace, he felt defeated as some of his fans assumed he was working on his videos.

JiDion quit his job at Lowa’s and started serving at Uber Eats. Working for Uber, he got more time to himself and was able to prepare content and perfect his craft.

He started putting more effort into his content, and the rest is history; he has established himself as one of the biggest personalities on the internet.
