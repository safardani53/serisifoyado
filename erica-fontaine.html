Early Life & Education

Erica Fontaine was born on July 22, 1998, in Gaithersburg, Virginia. She is the daughter of her proud parents: Donal Fontaine and Lauri Fontaine.

As of December 2023, Erica is 25 years old.

Nothing much about her parents is known, except they were supportive of her career choices.

In her interviews during her college days, Fontaine said that she is more passionate about a stage for gymnastics & becoming an influential person rather than graduating only.

She is an attendant of Gaithersburg High School, where she sowed seeds of a gymnastic career that sprout out during her time at WVU.

Not only did Erica graduate from Western Virginia University, but she also fulfilled her dreams. She now stands as an Instagram model & fitness guru, taking her gymnastics side by side.

According to her LinkedIn, she obtained a degree in strategic communication and advertising in 2020 from Western Virginia University.

Gymnastic Career

Erica started her career before joining the University of West Virginia and was on her high school gymnastics team.

She was doing well, but her career took a flight after joining Hills Gymnastics. From that point, everybody started taking her seriously as a professional gymnast.

Many might not know that Erica also qualified for Junior Olympics thrice but unfortunately couldn’t win the medal.

But the best quality of Fontaine is that she never gave up despite failing many times. 

She finished 2018 with an average of 9.761 points and 9.75 on the beam.

It was her time to shine in West Virginia for all four years, but she was kept out of the match in the first year due to injury.

And when she was doing better and everyone appreciated her, COVID happened in 2020, and the season was cut short.

As a result, she could not perform in her final year and finished high school with just a degree in strategic communication and advertising.

She says the best advice she has ever received from someone before getting fame was-

“Your mistakes don’t define you, but they will surely help you grow.”

Nonetheless, it has been her main mantra to calm herself down whenever she is under stress.

She still has a long way to go, and she is young, ambitious, and determined. Plus, there is no way she will not shine in the future.
