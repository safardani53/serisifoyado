Love Island's AJ Bunker says she's split from her boyfriend after finding out he's secretly a gay po

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 15, 2024

Love Island star AJ Bunker has split from her boyfriend after finding out he was a secret gay porn star.

The reality star beauty spoke to The Sun in an exclusive chat and revealed her horrifying ordeal after her former boyfriend “stole thousands” from her and “lied about his identity.”

AJ admits she’d been unlucky in love and failed to find a connection on Love Island, but her recent boyfriend seemed like “the dream,” until she found out the truth.

The star, who dated her boyfriend for three months, explained that he’d admitted to lying about owning his house, having a car, a dog, his profession and “lied her into bed.”

AJ said she’d discovered he was a gay porn star and it later transpired he’d asked her gay best friend to send him photos.

Explaining how they met, AJ shared: “We met at an event and he told me he was a model and had a football coaching company.

“I was actually chatting to someone before this but called things off because I was getting to know this guy. He seemed like an absolute dream. He told me he had a car, a dog, a lovely neighbour down the road who he’d go and visit, and he said he had his own place.

“He told me he wrote songs for Sony too and he seemed like the perfect guy and it was too good to be true.”

AJ, who admitted said she hadn’t slept with anyone for two years before him, said alarm bells started ringing when she went to his flat and “something didn’t feel right.”

She continued: “I put his address to find out where he lived and the YMCA came up. I then went to look for his business and put it in Companies House, nothing came up.

“He said he’d written a particular song for One Republic and again nothing came up. It was traumatising.”

The star explained that it was at her recent birthday when she confronted him about his lies and he admitted he hadn’t been truthful.

She said: “He told me he’d lied because he didn’t think I’d give him the time of the day if he’d told me the truth.

“He said he’d fallen in love with me, but he said he’d lied about having a dog and only said it because I’d told him I loved animals. He admitted he didn’t have a car and didn’t even have a license.

“By this point I was traumatised and called it a day. I trusted him at first because why would I ever have questioned it. I trusted him.”

After she’d ended her relationship AJ explained that she blocked him on every platform so that he couldn’t contact her.

“He would come with me to events and got introduced to all the people in my industry, like producers and influencers and celebrities,” AJ added, saying he even got signed to her agent.

The situation escalated for the star as she explained he “stole thousands” from her and her agent.

Then one day AJ got a call from a friend telling her to search a name on the internet which she did, only to discover her boyfriend was a gay porn star.

AJ continued: “It was extreme porn. It was all with men and I could see as clear as day that it was him.

“I was absolutely mortified and I didn’t think it could get any worse and then I discovered that. He’d even asked one of my gay friends to send him photos.”

The star said he’d also left a girls’ thong in her house which she discovered when moving his things out of her home.

“My friends sent me a picture of an ugly thong rolled up in a ball and put into his hoodie. I couldn’t believe he’d been bringing underwear to my house and leaving it there.”

AJ admits she’s been left “terrified” by the situation and said he’d “used her” to elevate himself into the public eye.

She said: “He’d come with me to events and be so possessive and controlling.”
