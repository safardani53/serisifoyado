What was Tina Turner's net worth?

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, May 3, 2024

TINA Turner was a legendary performer who was also known as the "Queen of Rock 'n' Roll."

Turner amassed a massive amount of wealth during her years as a musical performer before dying at the age of 83 on May 24, 2023.

What is Tina Turner's net worth?

The superstar singer is believed to have had a net worth of about $250million.

On October 6, 2021, it was reported that Turner sold the rights to her music catalogue, name, image, and likeness to music publishing company BMG.

The sale went for around $50million.

Turner said that her music was in "reliable hands," according to BBC.

Turner was one of the best-selling recording artists of all time and had 12 Grammy wins.

She became "one of the most commercially-successful international female rock stars to date," read her IMDb page.

"Her sultry, powerful voice, her incredible legs, her time-tested beauty and her unforgettable story all contribute to her legendary status."

The Associated Press wrote about Turner: "Few if any could ever match Turner as a performer."

"Both regal and raw, she was one of the supreme vocalists of the 20th century - a model of inspiration for Mick Jagger and countless others."

What are some of Tina Turner's most popular songs?

Tina Turner is known for making hit after hit throughout her career.

With 12 Grammy Awards, Turner sold over 100million records in her lifetime.

One of her hit songs, River Deep - Mountain High, released in 1966, is Rolling Stone's number 33 song on the 500 Greatest Songs of All Time list.

Turner took the world by storm in the 1980s with hit songs like What's Love Got to Do with It, Let's Stay Together, and I Don't Wanna Lose You.

Turner had a total of 10 studio albums, two live albums, and two soundtrack albums.

In 2020, Turner released a remix to What's Love Got to Do with It with EDM star Kygo.

How did Tina Turner die?

Tina Turner was announced dead on May 24, 2023, at the age of 83.

"Tina Turner, the 'Queen of Rock'n Roll' has died peacefully today at the age of 83 after a long illness in her home in Kusnacht near Zurich, Switzerland," her rep said in a statement.

In March 2023, Tina Turner looked back on her life in a documentary film Tina released by HBO.

Battling ill health during filming, she discussed how she wanted to enter the final chapter of her life out of the spotlight.

Tina suffered PTSD from the domestic abuse inflicted by her first husband, Ike Turner.

Ike died of an accidental drug overdose in 2007.

“For a long time I did hate Ike, I have to say that," Turner said in the film.

"But then, after he died, I really realized that he was an ill person. He did get me started and he was good to me in the beginning."

“But when do you stop being proud? I mean, when do you, how do you bow out slowly? Just go away?”

Turner was happily married to her second husband, Erwin Bach, since 2013.

The details of her life were also chronicled in her 1986 autobiography, I, Tina, and in the 1993 biopic What’s Love Got To Do With It? with Angela Bassett as Tina.
