Bella Thorne: ENGAGED to Gregg Sulkin?!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, May 1, 2024

Usually, when we bring you Bella Thorne-related news it means a new photo of Bella in a bikini has popped up on her Instagram page and there is cause for rejoicing.

Alas, today we’re not delivering tidings of new Thorne porn, but instead we have a somewhat unsettling update about Bella’s love life:

As you may have heard, Bella is dating Gregg Sulkin. 

She’s 18 and he’s 23, which doesn’t seem like too extreme of an age difference until you think about it in non-celeb terms.

Imagine you’re a year out of college and one of your friends starts dating a high school senior. Now you’re cringing.

Anyway, according to Hollywood Life, Bella was running errands in West Hollywood yesterday, and several sources claim that the actress/Instagram model was sporting a giant rock on her left ring finger.

Bella has yet to confirm that she’s engaged, but she’s been dating Gregg for quite some time (yes, since before she was "legal" under California state law), and this isn’t the first time we’ve heard engagement rumors about the couple.

The age difference is a tad unseemly, but people grow up fast in Hollywood, and we know that Gregg’s not the top to stop Bella from posting photos of her butt on Instagram, so we reluctantly support this union. Mazel tov!
