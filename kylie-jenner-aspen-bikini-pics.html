Flaunts Her Figure While Soaking In Pool Hollywood Life

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, March 9, 2024

Kylie Jenner sure set pulses racing for her 206 million Instagram followers in new Instagram bikini photos she shared on Dec. 30. The camera was pointed down at the 23-year-old as she sat waist-deep in an indoor pool after arriving in Aspen, CO for New Year’s. Her flesh-colored tiny triangle bikini top could barely contain her curves, as the rest of her famous hourglass figure was under the water line. Kylie oozed sex appeal with a smoldering expression on her face, which was made up perfectly with a flawless makeup job.

Kylie definitely wasn’t going to be putting her gorgeous face underwater, as she had a rose colored lip, pink cheeks and a smokey eye as she gazed into the camera. Her eyebrows were shaped to perfection, and she wore her red locks pulled back in a tight bun with a side part. The cosmetics mogul accessorized with a pair of sunglasses atop her head and the frames matched the color of her nude bikini top perfectly. Ky added some small round hoop earrings and a delicate diamond choker necklace to top off her pool pose. In less than four hours, the IG photo amassed over six million likes.

Kylie later shared three more photos of her taken in the pool, which appeared to be indoors with a lush green wall of plants next to it and some colorful inflatable beach balls in the water. She captioned the photos “swimming into 2021” after only leaving a snowflake emoji in the caption of her first snapshot. This time around, Kylie really posed her heart out for her fans, and nearly had a wardrobe malfunction as the very bottom of her bikini top became wet, and the fabric seemed like it could become see-through if the rest of her top was fully soaked.

Ky flashed a closed mouth smile in one snapshot, while tilting her head back and opening her lips for a sultry expression in another picture. In the third photo, the mother of one went all out with her sex appeal. She put on her sunglasses and closed her eyes, while arching her back and pushing her ample chest forward in the water.  Her itty bitty string bikini bottom could be seen through the water, as well as the skin of her nearly bare derriere.

The photos literally caused fans’ jaws to drop, at least according to her followers. @itsjohnnycyrus commented, “my mouth dropped!!” next to Kylie’s original bikini top photo, as user @tiffanysorya wrote, “*Jaw drop*.” Fan @chaneldijon gushed, “Ohhhh my goddddd” at how incredible Kylie looked in the snapshot.

Kylie and her supermodel sister Kendall, 25, just arrived in Aspen on Dec. 29, where they plan to spend New Years. The sisters immediately hit up a local sporting goods store where they were able to snap up black Dior snowboards, as well as bindings and boots. The ladies then put them to good use as they hit the slopes on Dec. 30, where Kendall shared an IG stories video of the pair smoothly snowboarding and showing off their athletic skills. Kylie donned a black ski jacket and pants, while Kendall went with a metallic silver jacket. Both sisters made sure to be safe in black ski helmets that hid their famous faces. Now we can’t wait to see what fun the Jenner sisters have in store for New Year’s Eve!
