Who is Fallon Sherrocks partner Cameron Menzies?

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 3, 2024

DATING and darts go hand-in-hand for Fallon Sherrock who is in a relationship with fellow thrower Cameron Menzies.

But who is Cameron Menzies and when did the pair meet? - here’s everything you need to know.

Who is Fallon Sherrock’s partner Cameron Menzies?

Cameron Menzies, born June 27, 1989, is a Scottish darts player who plays in Professional Darts Corporation (PDC) events.

He is also dating fellow thrower Fallon Sherrock.

Sherrock shot to fame in 2019 when she became the first female to win a match at the PDC World Championship.

She also earned the nickname "Queen of the Palace" for her darts ability.

Glasgow-born ace Menzies first played darts on the BDO circuit in 2006, playing in the Gibraltar Open. 

However, he did not emerge to prominence until 2016 when he won the British Open.

Menzies later went on to play in the 2016 World Masters but suffered a loss to Irish darts player John O'Shea.

The following year, in 2017, he became the first Scottish player to win the Scottish Open in 10 years - when Gary Anderson won it.

His career highlights also include qualifying for the 2017 BDO World Trophy through the regional qualifiers, reaching the semi-finals of the 2017 World Masters, and reaching the final stages of the Grand Slam of Darts (staged by PDC).

Away from the world of darts, to make ends meet, Menzies works shifts as a plumber.

But balancing jobs is not without its problems, with Menzies telling SunSport: "I had issues with my hands at the Players Championship.

"It happens after I use certain power tools which vibrate. My hands were a bit numb-ish."

Where did Fallon Sherrock and Cameron Menzies meet?

With both boasting a passion for darts, it comes as no surprise that the pair first met each other while playing in an online darts league, competing in a doubles team.

Following their initial online encounter, Menzies revealed that he wooed Fallon over - and ‘’it started off with a KFC’’.

He said: “I had known Fallon for years in darts and played doubles with her years ago.

''We met in an online league in Southampton.

‘’The rest is history as they say. It started off with a KFC.’’

Since finding romance, Menzies has admitted that his partner has made him a better player, adding: “In our house, she’s still the best player.

"She has made my darts better. If I wasn't with Fallon I'd still be a decent darts player, but she has definitely made me a better player. 

“She has helped my darts career massively. We have been together for two and a half years and in that time my darts has gone one way.

"Her mindset is miles better than mine. She is so composed about everything. What she goes through and does for this game is mental.

"She is brilliant. I am her biggest fan. I sometimes tell her as a person she is not doing this right but as a player she is amazing.”
