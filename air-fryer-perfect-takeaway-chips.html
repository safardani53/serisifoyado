Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, February 9, 2024

NOTHING quite hits the spot like a takeaway when you have the craving, especially chips when they are done well.

A woman has revealed how she managed to recreate the perfect takeaway chips in her Air Fryer in just 15 minutes.

TikTok user Khadija, who posts under @traveleatrepeatuk, uploaded a video showing her easy recipe.

She explained: “Let's make some salt and pepper Chinese style chips in the Air Fryer.”

The foodie showed how you need frozen chips, 1 tbsp of minced garlic, an onion and pepper.

Khadija added: “Be sure to cut the onions and pepper a bit thick so they don’t burn while the chips cook.”

More on air fryers

Next you need to add 1tbsp of sesame oil, 1tsp dark soy, 1tbsp vinegar, half tsp salt, 1 tsp black pepper, 1tsp MSG (optional).

She continued that you should mix all the ingredients very well and add to the Air Fryer for 15 minutes at 200C and shake halfway through.

Many people were impressed with the recipe, and her video has racked up over 6,000 likes.

One said: “Literally just followed this recipe was amazing!”
