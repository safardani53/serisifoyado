Who is Tracy Romulus' husband Ray Romulus?

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, June 4, 2024

CHIEF Financial Officer, Tracy Romulus, is known for her work with Kim Kardashian West Brands.

Her husband, Ray Romulus, is equally as talented in the business world.

Who is Tracy Romulus' husband Ray Romulus?

Ray Romulus, 32, is a rapper and well-known music producer.

He has worked with big names like Kanye West, Mariah Carey, Cardi B, Justin Bieber, Iggy Azalea, Lil Yachty and Bruno Mars.

His work on Bruno Mars‘ song, That’s What I Like, led to two Grammys in 2018: Song of the Year and Best R&B Song.

Ray is also a part of the R&B production group, The Stereotypes, as a songwriter and producer.

The Stereotypes have collaborated on Sevyn Streeter’s song, Before I Do, and Fifth Harmony’s hit, Deliver.

The mega-producer has nearly 27.4k followers on Instagram.

Who is Tracy Romulus?

Tracy Romulus, 43, is a businesswoman and has been the Chief Financial Officer of Kim Kardashian West Brands since 2017.

She has been by Kim Kardashian's side as the reality-star launched her brands: Skims, KKW Beauty, and KKW Fragrance.

Tracy graduated from the Fashion Institute of Technology for fashion design in 2000.

In a 2021 interview with People, Tracy spoke of her partnership with Kim: "What makes work so incredibly fulfilling for me is that I get to work alongside such brilliant, inspiring, creative women — like Kim and Kris Jenner — and the most talented, hardest working team in the world. I feel incredibly blessed."

The businesswoman has accumulated over 500k followers.

How many children do Tracy and Ray have?

Tracy and Ray Romulus, have been married since 2014.

The couple are parents to three children; Ryan, Remi, and Raf.

The CFO often posts pictures of her family to her social media pages.
