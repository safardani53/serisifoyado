KELLY Clarkson has flaunted her weight loss in a stunning gold gown on her talk show on Monday.

The Breakaway singer, 41, has sparked concern after dropping several dress sizes over the last few months.

Kelly brushed the controversy aside on Monday as she hosted an episode of The Kelly Clarkson Show with singing legend, Barry Manilow.

The mother of two looked incredible in a metallic dress with a plunging neckline and tiered skirt.

She teamed the garment with a pair of towering black heels and gold hoop earrings.

On Friday's episode of The Kelly Clarkson Show, the TV personality went head to head with her vocal director Jessi Collins for a festive game of Christmas Carol Finish The Lyric.

The singer and her colleague listened to producers read out a lyric from a classic Christmas tune, and the quickest to push the red buzzer and reveal the song won the round.

In one round, Kelly guessed Away In A Manger so quickly that it left Jessi stunned and "sweaty."

Kelly responded: "I'm cold! In this building, it's so cold."

Her friend retorted: "I know but you said that so fast!"

"I don't even need to wear Spanx anymore, I just wear them for warmth like thermals," the mother of two revealed.

"But let's just give a high five, I don't have to wear Spanx anymore if I don't want to," Kelly bragged.

"Whatever, life is good," she closed.

Last month, the host admitted that she's been loving losing weight.
