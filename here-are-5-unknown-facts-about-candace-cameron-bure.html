Here Are 5 Unknown Facts About Candace Cameron Bure

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, March 18, 2024

Candace Cameron Bure has departed the Hallmark Channel after 14 years of service. She announced her departure in April 2022 and recently discussed her ambitions to work with the Great American Family and its sibling organization, Great American Living. The actress has appeared in over 20 Hallmark Channel original films, most of which have been Christmas specials. While she has left the media channel to work for a new company, fans will see her again in Christmas movies.

Cameron Bure has revealed that her first production with Great American Country Media will be a Christmas film, which will release in November of this year. She will star as well as executive produce in the film, titled A Christmas…Present. As a result, today we will look at some lesser-known facts about the actress in order to provide fans with a more complete picture of Candace Cameron Bure. Candace Cameron Bure’s accomplishments range from overcoming depression to becoming a famous author. Here are five facts about her that you should be aware of.

1) She began her career as Kirk Cameron’s sister.

Candace Cameron Bure, who was born in Panorama City, Los Angeles, California to Robert and Barbara Cameron, began her on-screen career at a young age, appearing in commercials for businesses such as Cabbage Patch Dolls and KFC. She made her debut appearance in the drama series St. Elsewhere in 1982. Following that, she featured in shows such as Who’s The Boss?, Punky Brewster, TJ Hooker, and Growing Pain.

Growing Pain was meaningful to her since she co-starred with her brother Kirk Cameron. While having a brother in the profession was helpful, Candace was skilled enough to stand on her own. 2) Candace Cameron Bure is a novelist.

Candace Cameron Bure is a published novelist in addition to her considerable acting career in television films. She has written many novels, one of which became a New York Times bestseller. Her writings mostly convey the tale of her personal life and are intended to guide readers through her experiences.

Among her many works are Reshaping It All: Motivation for Physical and Spiritual Fitness (2011), Balancing It All: My Story of Juggling Priorities and Purpose (2014), Dancing Through Life: Steps of Courage and Conviction (2015), and Kind is the New Classy: The Power of Living Graciously (2016). 3) She is enjoying the “happily ever after” that her movies depict.

Candace Cameron Bure is a fan favorite as one of the principal actresses in Hallmark’s Christmas special movie every year. Cameron, like the feel-good romance flicks she starred in, has been married to Valeri Bure for over 23 years. In 1994, the actress met the Russian-born hockey player during a charity tournament in Ontario. 4) She is a co-owner of a prestigious winery.

Candace Cameron Bure and her husband Valeri Bure founded their vineyard in 2006. Valeri’s passion for wines led to the development of the Bure Family Wines when a back accident interrupted his hockey career. Bure Family Wines creates limited-edition wines such as the Majesty Blend, Nuit Blanche, and Duration. Candace Cameron Bure may have been the face that brightened many fans’ days with her various feel-good endeavors, but she had a hard spell in her early twenties. In several interviews, the actress discussed her depression and the eating disorder that accompanied it.
