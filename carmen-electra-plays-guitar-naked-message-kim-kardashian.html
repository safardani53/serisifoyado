The actress took to the platform on Thursday to share the major throwbacks with her 1.7 million followers.

The first image in the compilation was the model's April 2003 Playboy Magazine cover.

CARMEN FOR SKIMS

Back in July, Carmen and Jenny McCarthy paired up with Kim Kardashian's brand Skims for a sexy photoshoot.

In one of the photos, the Good Burger star mimicked her 2003 Playboy shoot by posing with a guitar similarly while wearing a Skims one-piece bathing suit.

The 50 and 51-year-old actresses were the stars of the shapewear brand's marketing campaign.

The Meet The Spartans alum posted a video of her in Skims on Instagram to promote Kim's, 42, new swimwear line.

The blondes were both wearing matching tiny black string bikinis that showed off their amazing bodies and underboob.

The superstars were standing in front of a red tile wall and a bright red car.

Each was armed with a sudsy red sponge as they pretended to wash the car, but focused on washing themselves.

At one point, Jenny was on the ground scrubbing the floor while Carmen washed the car behind her.

They both seductively looked into the camera like they were posing for Playboy.

Jenny shared the snaps online and added: "I couldn't be more excited to be a part of SKIMS' latest campaign.

"The energy on set was incredible, especially shooting alongside Carmen, who looks amazing!"

"It felt like we were back in the '90s, and years later we're still having fun!"

Carmen captioned her post: "It’s a wet hot summer in @SKIMS Swim with me and @jennymccarthy #SKIMS."

The pair have known each other for years after finding fame with MTV and Playboy modeling in the 1990s.

Carmen also starred in the popular show Baywatch in the late 1980s.
