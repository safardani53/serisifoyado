What is Kelly Osbourne's net worth?

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 2, 2024

KELLY Osbourne is no longer known as just the daughter of Ozzy and Sharon Osbourne.

The 38-year-old has paved her own career in the entertainment industry as an actress, fashion designer, model, singer, and television personality.

What is Kelly Osbourne's net worth?

Kelly Osbourne became a household name after appearing on the MTV reality show, The Osbournes, alongside her famous family members.

Since then, she has skyrocketed to fame and become known as more than just Ozzy and Sharon's daughter.

Due to her success, she has been able to amass an extensive net worth estimated to be around $16million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

This number comes from record sales, and sponsorship deals, among other things.

In 2009, Kelly appeared on ABC's Dancing with the Stars and later became a panelist and presenter on the E! network's, Fashion Police.

She's also appeared as a judge on shows like America's Next Top Model, RuPaul's Drag Race, Project Runway: Junior, and Australia's Got Talent.

Was Kelly Osbourne in rehab?

Over the years, Kelly publicly struggled with alcohol addiction, and it was announced on October 18, 2021, that she had checked into rehab in Austin, Texas.

The move came just one month after she admitted to having a relapse on her Instagram stories and opened up about previous experiences with heroin and painkillers during an interview with Red Table Talk.
