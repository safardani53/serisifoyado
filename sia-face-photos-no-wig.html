Sia Without Her Signature Wig: A History

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, February 3, 2024

WHEN SHE CELEBRATED CAMPING

with Jay Duplass in L.A. in October 2018.

WHEN SHE HUNG OUT WITH JASON BATEMAN

at Netflix's Ozark Tastemakers event in August 2018.

WHEN SHE AND KATE HUDSON CHANNELED THEIR INNER MODELS

at an Amazon Studios Holiday Party in December 2017.

WHEN SHE ARRIVED AT THE AIRPORT LIKE IT WAS NBD

but really, all eyes were on the singer's glowing complexion.

WHEN SHE POSED FOR MAKEUP ARTIST TONYA BREWER

and let her masked self (and breast) loose, as seen on Brewer's Instagram.

WHEN SHE AND MARY J. BLIGE CAUGHT UP

and looked stunning at Spotify's Creators Party in February 2016.

WHEN SHE RUBBED ELBOWS WITH CHRISTIAN SIRIANO

at the designer's 2015 clothing collection preview, no less.

WHEN SHE ATTENDED A 2015 GOLDEN GLOBES VIEWING PARTY

and coincidentally wore a similar lipstick shade as actress Kathryn Hahn.

WHEN SHE SHOWED HER SUPPORT FOR THE LGBT COMMUNITY

during a performance at the Democratic National Committee LGBT Gala in June 2014.

WHEN HER FAUX BANGS WERE NOWHERE TO BE SEEN

during a meet up with producer Erik Anders Lang, Orlando Bloom and Rashida Jones at the Hammer Museum's 12th Annual Gala.

WHEN SHE BELTED OUT A SONG

and there were no wigs in sight, at the Hammer Museum's 12th Annual Gala.

WHEN SHE TOOK MACKENZIE ZIEGLER TO SEE 2014'S ANNIE

and opted for a messy bun for maximum views.

AND WHEN SHE POSED SOLO

and inspired us to rock a bold red lip for the rest of our lives.

WHEN SHE AND HEIDI KLUM EMBRACED LIKE OLD FRIENDS

at the 23rd Annual Elton John AIDS Foundation Academy Awards Viewing Party.

WHEN SHE ENTHUSIASTICALLY MADE A BID

and photographers got a closer look at her low ponytail, at The L.A. Gay & Lesbian Center's Annual "An Evening with Women" event in May 2014.

WHEN SHE MADE A MAJOR STYLE STATEMENT

in a classic white ensemble at the GEMS' 2015 Love Revolution Gala.
