Helen Flanagan sizzles in red dress for Ladies Day at Aintree Racecourse

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, February 18, 2024

HELEN Flanagan stunned fans in a gorgeous red dress as she got ready for a glamorous day out at the races.

The ex-Corrie star, 32, who recently underwent a secret boob job, showed off her look with fans on social media.

The actress headed out for the Ladies Day at the Randox Grand National Festival at Aintree Racecourse, in Liverpool.

The mum-of-three was pictured in a floor-length red dress that hung off one shoulder.

The stunning outfit had a frilled skirt at the bottom and a suited-up button top.

Helen also wore a gorgeous black and white headpiece which she paired with a black and silver clutch bag and a pair of black stiletto heels.

She finished it off with a pair of long gold earings and had her hair pulled back in a tight bun.

She glammed up for the day out which saw many famous faces head to the racecourse.

TV presenter Gethin Jones, fellow Corrie star Ellie Leach, and actress Sarah Jayne Dunn were all spotted at the races.

Helen’s new snaps come after the blonde beauty returned from a family holiday in Dubai.

She showed off her stunning figure as she hit the beach at the five-star Le Royal Meridien resort.

The TV star tied her hair up and cooled off with a dip in the water during some brief solo time in the Middle East.

Helen returned home last weekend and paid tribute to the two luxury hotels she and her family stayed at on their holiday.

She wrote on Instagram: "Lovely Easter stay @leroyalmeridiendubai @grosvenorhouse stayed at this hotel since I’ve been 19 and I have the best memories here x "

"My kids have been here since babies and all know it so well x I’ve had all my baby moon breaks here, my gender reveal with Delilah, New Years etc it really is a home from home for me.

"It’s my favourite hotel in Dubai and my kids just love it and are obsessed with the kids club and making friends with other kids."

Helen shares her children – Matilda, seven, Delilah, four, and two-year-old Charlie – with her ex-fiance Scott Sinclair.

Earlier this week we told how Helen made her final decision about her relationship with Scott after seeking advice from celeb pal Carol Vorderman.

It was reported earlier this month that Helen, 32, and Scott, 34, were growing close again after their shock split last year.

However The Sun revealed that actress Helen is sticking to her guns right now - thanks to some words of wisdom from her I'm a Celebrity South Africa co-star Carol.

An insider told us: "Helen was at a crossroads in her relationship when she went into the jungle this time around. 

"Carol became a kind of mother figure to the other campmates and Helen found herself opening up to her.
