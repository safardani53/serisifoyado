SiriusXM will also welcome Team Coco’s personnel. Several podcasts have been bought in recent months by firms such as Amazon, Spotify, and others. “We are happy that Conan continues to produce his phenomenally popular podcast and will now extend his position to executive produce an exclusive Team Coco radio station for Sirius XM.” Conan has developed an incredible brand and structure at Team Coco, and he has a proven track record of discovering and releasing fascinating and addicting podcasts.
