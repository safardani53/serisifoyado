Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 27, 2024

SISTER Wives daughter Gwendlyn Brown called her "girl crush" Zendaya "the love of my life" after coming out as bisexual.

The 19-year-old is the daughter of Christine and Kody Brown as the family's life has been showcased on the popular TLC reality series.

Gwendlyn took to her Instagram Story to hop on the "share a pic of" trend when one fan asked her to show off her "girl crush."

The reality star proceeded to share a snap of Zendaya, 24, looking glamorous as she wrote over the pic: "Oh you mean the love of my life?"

Back in August, Gwendlyn took to her Twitter bio to come out as she said: "I’m your basic, average girl and I’m here to save the world. BLACK LIVES MATTER • Raging Bisexual • she/her.”

While the family's religion, Apostolic United Brethren - a fundamentalist mormon sect - does not believe in same-sex marriage, Gwendlyn received plenty of support from her loved ones.

Gwendlyn’s older sister Mykelti shared her support during a Facebook Live LuLaRoe clothing sale.

When a fan asked if Mykelti is “super proud of Gwen for coming out as bisexual,” she responded, “of course I am!”

Gwendlyn has continued to be open about her life as earlier this year she also revealed to her fans that she previously suffered from anorexia.

After Twitter user admitted to disliking exercising and suggested it could possibly stem from often being told that when they were a kid that they should lose weight, Gwendlyn jumped in to say that she dealt with similar issues when she was younger.

The TLC star said: “I was a fat kid and actually developed anorexia at a v young age bc of this.

“Now I have a bad relationship w food but I’m skinny. ”

She added: “I’m not lying btw it took me a while to lose my baby fat and I got bullied for it in preschool and through to second grade.

“Fortunately I was thinner when the show started so no fans bullied me baby gwendlyn wouldn’t be able to take it”

Three years prior to Gwendlyn coming out, Kody's other daughter with wife Meri, Mariah, also came out - she's now engaged to fiancée Audrey Kriss.
