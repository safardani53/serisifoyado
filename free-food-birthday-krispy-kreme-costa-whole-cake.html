Were so savvy when it comes to bagging free food - you can get a free lunch AND a 5-inch cake, he

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, August 24, 2024

A SAVVY COUPLE has revealed all the free stuff you can nab on your birthday - including a free burrito, beauty products, and an entire five-inch cake. 

Hiren and Maggie took to TikTok to share the offers they managed to claim at Bluewater Shopping Centre. 

The pair, known online as @insideourrelationship, regularly shared life updates with their 29,300 followers. 

In a new reel, Maggie said: “This is all the free stuff you can get when it’s your birthday, part two.”

First up, the pair headed to their local Lindt shop. Hiren explained: “We couldn’t believe this one. Lindt give you a full box of chocolates for your birthday.”

Although Maggie was more impressed by the next offer, adding: “I think this one might be even better though. You get a free five-inch cake, a full cake, from Lola’s Cupcakes on your birthday.”

In the comments, the pair confirmed you do have to sign up at least three months before your birthday to claim this offer. 

Hiren continued: “Next up is Boost, where you can basically pick any drink from their menu for absolutely free. 

“We went for some sort of Berry one, and it was so good. 

“Hotel Chocolat give you a £5 voucher on your birthday so we found these little bunnies which were £6.50, so we just paid £1.50 for them.

“Costa give you a free sweet treat. Maggie loves the millionaire shortbread from there so we had to go and get that.”

Maggie added: “This was another banging one we found. In Rituals, you can basically get a full-size shower oil completely free on your birthday.

“The Body Shop give you £5 off and we found this mango hand cream which was £6  so we ended up spending £1 on that. 

“Next up is Tortilla, where you get a free wrap, and oh my did she fill it to the max. 

“This was so tasty and completely free. 

“And in case you still weren’t full after all of that, Krispy Kreme give you a free doughnut of your choice. 

“And for all your craft lovers, you get £5 off from Hobbycraft on your birthday.”

Below the video, they explained how many of the schemes work, adding: “You have to sign up to their newsletters to get the free goodies to prove it’s your bday.

“You can collect your free goodies from three to 30 days after your birthday (depending on the shop) so you don’t have to go on your actual bday.”

Fans loved the pair’s savvy finds, with the video gaining more than 215,400 likes and 1.7 million views. 

In the comments, other TikTok users shared their thoughts, with one writing: “I’M SO MAD THIS VIDEO DIDN’T SHOW UP BEFORE MY BIRTHDAY.”
