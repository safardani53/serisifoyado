The 22 Best Places to Buy Underwear of 2024

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, February 28, 2024

Underwear shopping in-store is one thing, but online is a whole different experience. While it’s actually more convenient and enjoyable to buy undies online, feeling confident in the size, fit, and material can get a little tricky, so finding a brand or retailer that is reliable can be a total game-changer. 

“There’s nothing better than finding a brand you know, trust, and love — a brand you can turn to time and time again when underwear shopping,” Jonathan Shokrian, the CEO of MeUndies, tells PEOPLE. Since underwear is something you wear every single day, you want to know for sure that you’re getting a pair you’ll love, so once you find your favorite place to buy underwear, it’s worth going back to when you're ready to stock up on more. 

To find the best places to buy underwear, Shokrian says to focus on fabric, fit, and customer experience (this includes that pesky return policy) to narrow down your search and find a reliable brand you love — and we couldn’t agree more. But, with so many underwear brands out there, you’re going to be sifting through the internet’s intimates for quite some time, so we decided to do it for you. After researching nearly 50 brands and retailers, we found the best places to buy underwear for all styles, sizes, and budgets.

How to Pick the Right Place to Buy Underwear

Material

When shopping for underwear, Dr. Somi Javaid, a board-certified OBGYN physician and surgeon, says to consider materials that are sweat-wicking, breathable, and temperature-regulating. “These characteristics will promote enhanced comfort and reduce the risk of adverse health concerns,” she explains. It’s also important that underwear is comfortable, so double-check that the underwear has plenty of stretch and will fit well without digging into or pinching your body. 

In addition to the health aspect, the material also matters when shopping for underwear to wear under specific clothing. For example, if you need something seamless — such as those offered at Harper Wilde, Girlfriend Collective, and Skims — look for no-show styles that are constructed from breathable and stretchy nylon material. 

Styles 

Finding the best places to buy underwear typically means finding options with the styles you love. Most underwear brands will offer a variety of styles, including briefs, thongs, bikinis, and shorts. Some — such as Spanx and Yitty — will also feature designs that double as shapewear for underwear that support, smooths, and sculpts the body under clothing. 

Sizes and Fit 

It’s so important that underwear fits well. Not only does this make for a more comfortable experience and prevent unwanted visible panty lines, but it also impacts health. When shopping for underwear online, make sure the brands you browse carry styles in your size, and if you aren’t sure about the fit, consider the return policy to ensure you can exchange it for a different size. 

Return Policy

Speaking of the return policy, this is also important when looking at places to buy underwear. In many situations, underwear return policies aren’t that great, and some are even final sale. However, many stores allow you to return unworn and unwashed undergarments with tags still on, so make note of this when shopping and potentially trying on pieces at home. 

Harper Wilde has one of the best return policies on our list. As an undergarments brand, it knows that shopping for underwear online is a bit challenging, especially if you don’t know your size. So, with that in mind, Harper Wilde allows customers to return their first pair of underwear in each style for a full refund or store credit within 90 days of fulfillment. After that, all underwear must be in its original packaging and unworn in order to return. 

Frequently Asked Questions

  • When should I buy underwear?

    Durable and high-quality underwear should last you between one and two years. However, this all depends on how you care for your undies and how much wear and tear they experience. And, in many cases, you can get away with wearing underwear for much longer. If your underwear starts to fray or becomes uncomfortable or too big due to being stretched out, it’s worth replacing for a pair that will stay in place. But, if your undies still fit well and don’t twist or have any damage, you can continue to wear them long after the one to two-year period. 


    As far as the best time to shop for underwear is concerned, you can always find great deals from popular underwear brands during certain sale seasons. Black Friday is a good time to stock up on more splurge-worthy brands, as many offer discounts during the sale event. Labor Day, Boxing Day, and other sale weekends like Memorial Day Weekend are also good shopping days. Additionally, you can find sales both on Amazon marketplace and individual brand websites during Amazon Prime Day. 

  • What kind of underwear is most comfortable?

    Underwear style is all about personal preference, however, the material makes a major difference in overall comfort. When shopping for the most comfortable underwear, Shokrian says to look for “high-quality underwear that uses the best fabrics in the business — something stretchy, soft, and breathable yet also durable.” Fit and flexibility are also worth considering as “you want your underwear to move with you,” Shokrian adds.  


    When researching the best places to buy underwear, we took comfort seriously and looked for brands that carry ultra-comfy styles. However, if you want just a few options for comfy undies based on material and fit, we recommend Harper Wilde, Girlfriend Collective, MeUndies, Lively, and EBY. Cosabella is also a good choice for those looking for comfortable and soft lace styles.

  • How long should you keep underwear?

    “You should keep underwear as long as it still looks good and feels good on,” says Noa Arias and Shaula Yemini, the co-founders of Bloomers Intimates. They explain that, as long as underwear is good quality and you care for it properly, you should be able to get at least a year or two from each pair. To ensure you get the most out of your underwear, check the care instructions before tossing it into the wash, as some undergarments require hand washing while others can be washed in a washing machine if placed in a delicates bag.
